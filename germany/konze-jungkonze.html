The Jungkonze / Jungkontze branch Trendelburg & Stammen

A compound surname (“young Konze”) treated by the registers as a separate but related family, documented from 1755 with explicit Stammen roots. Whether it is a cadet branch of the Kontze line or a distinct family remains unresolved. The whole branch is now exactly dated (gap-closing reads of 6–7 June 2026).
