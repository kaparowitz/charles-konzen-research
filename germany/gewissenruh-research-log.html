Gewissenruh — Parish Research Log

51.623 N · 9.562 E · reg. 219001 · ~11.7 km NE of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmations swept 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Tiny Huguenot parish (~3 births/yr). No January 1835 baptism at all; the only window entry is female.

Confirmation register now also swept (24 Jun 2026). The Konfirmationen 1847–1855 (births 1833–1841) were read class-by-class in the Archion viewer: no surname with a Konze / *n-s-n skeleton anywhere, and the single window Carl — Johann Karl Jouquenal, b. 1 Aug 1834 — carries a Huguenot surname and a non-anchor birth date. Confirms the elimination at the leaf level.
