Liebenau (+ Grimelsheim) — Parish Research Log

51.496 N · 9.276 E · ~12.7 km SW of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026
ELIMINATED · confirmed on Archion (21 June 2026)

Map-changing finding (21 June 2026). The June 2026 sweep recorded Liebenau as a coverage hole — Archion holding the registers only to 1777. That is no longer true: Archion now carries Taufen 1830–1890 (register 219745), which covers January 1835. It was opened live and the 1835 window read.

The 1835 baptisms (Jahr 1835, Januar–März, Seite 27 — image 1462, entries 87–90) are all legitimate males with named fathers — Justus Klüte (b. Oct 1834, bapt. Jan 1835), Ludwig Wilhelm Winnefeld (b. 12 Jan 1835), Carl [Ackermann] (b. 15 Mar 1835), Johann Heinrich Taubel (b. Mar 1835). No Konze-pool surname, no illegitimate male, and no birth on or near 26 January 1835 (the nearest January birth is Winnefeld, 12 Jan, legitimate). Positively eliminated — now from the image, not merely flagged as an undigitised gap.
