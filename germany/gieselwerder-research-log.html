Oberweser / Gieselwerder — Parish Research Log

51.581 N · 9.522 E · reg. 219016 (Taufen) · reg. 219013 (Konfirmationen) · ~7.3 km east of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmation sweep added 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Oberweser’s Evangelical records are held under Gieselwerder. Jan–Feb 1835 baptisms: all males legitimate, no Konze-pool surname, no illegitimate male.

Confirmation sweep (24 June 2026): the Konfirmationen book (reg. 219013) was read leaf-by-leaf for the entire 1847–1855 classes (births ~1832–1841), covering the prime 1849 and 1850 cohorts of children born around Charles’s 26 Jan 1835. Every confirmand was checked for the forename Carl/Karl and for any Konze / *n-s-n / *n-z-n surname. Result: negative. The whole window holds exactly one Carl — Carl Friedrich Wilhelm Marunde, b. 21 March 1839 (1853 class), a local Marunde family, wrong surname and wrong birth year — and no Konze, Kontze, Gansen, Gonsen or any *n-s-n surname appears anywhere. The surname pool is entirely the recurring local Oberweser families (Marunde, Roll, Reuß, Nickel, Hennur, Vella, Schölla, Grafmann, Grovenmann, Lindner, Otto, Daus, Reitz, Müller, Masserschmidt). Unlike the other Hofgeismar confirmation reels, reg. 219013 is cleanly ordered (≈ Seite = 2 × image − 3914).
