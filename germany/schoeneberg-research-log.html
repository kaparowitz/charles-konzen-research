Schöneberg — Parish Research Log

51.578 N · 9.357 E · ~4.4 km west of Deisel · pre-1822 undigitised · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmation sweep 23 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Grandjot territory; no Konze. Pre-1822 records remain undigitised, but the post-1822 Schöneberg index returned no Konze entry.

Confirmation sweep complete (23 June 2026, Session 114). The 1847–1853 Konfirmationen classes in reg. 220012 were read leaf-by-leaf. No Carl is born in 1834 or 1835, and the 1848 and 1849 classes — the natural cohort for a boy born 26 Jan 1835 — contain no Carl at all. The four Carls that do appear (b. 1833, 1836, 1836, 1839) all carry local surnames (Siebert, Küthen, Grandjot, Wagner), none a Konze / *n·s·n* variant. This clears the previously jumble-blocked “Not yet” cell and strengthens the verdict.

Confirmation-register sweep — “any Carl, 1833–1841”

Part of the June 2026 widened confirmation-register sweep across the Kreis Hofgeismar — looking for any boy named Carl/Karl born 1833–1841, surname open, who would surface in the 1847–1855 confirmation classes. This is the parish-specific row; the cross-parish ledger, the full list of boys named Carl that turned up, and the method/reliability notes live on Parish search coverage.
