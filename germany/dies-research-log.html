Dies — Parish Research Log

50.345 N · 8.025 E · Rhein-Lahn-Kreis · ~135 km S · compiled 21 June 2026 · updated 27 June 2026 (Archion holdings check + Holzappel baptism-window search)
ELIMINATED

Dies is the small Rhein-Lahn-Kreis hamlet floated in 2020 as a Hessen-Nassau alternative reading of the marriage register’s “Deisse.” It lies ~135 km south of the Hofgeismar Kirchspiel-complex and carries no Konze/Kontze surname pool.

On 27 June 2026, when the confirmation window was swept for the southern alternatives, Dies was checked directly against Archion. Dies has no standalone Evangelical parish or register on Archion — an Archion Ortssuche for “Dies” returns a single, unrelated hit (an Einwohnerverzeichnis in Breitungen/St. Arnold, Sachsen-Anhalt), and no “Dies” Gemeinde appears in the Dekanat Diez parish tree. As a hamlet, Dies fell under a neighbouring Dekanat-Diez parish; the nearest town parish, Diez, was swept on the confirmation track (1847–1855) and is negative, and the 2020 AncestryProGenealogists search had already checked Holzappel and Freiendiez baptisms (the other “Deisse / Freundburg” readings) with no candidate. There is no “Dies” confirmation register to sweep; the toponym remains negative.

Baptism-window search (27 June 2026). Since Dies keeps no book of its own, the baptism window for a 1833–1841 birth was checked in Holzappel — the Hessen-Nassau parish that the 2020 search had already fixed on for the “Deisse / Dies” reading, and which covers the Dies-area hamlets (its Taufregister records Holzappel, Laurenburg, Scheidt and Horhausen births). Two complementary checks, both negative: (1) the parish’s alphabetical Namensregister 1817–1871 — the whole K column is local Lahn families (Kuhn, Kramb, Kalkofen, Kah, Kleyn, Knoll, Keis, Keuper, Kraus) with no Konze / Kontze, and there is no I/J leaf at all (so no Jansen / Jungkonze); (2) the prime year 1835 of the Taufregister 1817–1841, read directly — the births are local Nassau families (Herpel, Priester, Meier, Schaub, Vetter, Gall, Debusmann…), no Konze / Gansen surname and no matching Carl. This corroborates the AncestryProGenealogists 2020 Holzappel baptism search (also negative).

The southern (Hessen-Nassau) reading was eliminated on geography in 2020; this 2026 pass closes it on the confirmation track via the covering parishes.
