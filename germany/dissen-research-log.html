Dissen — Parish Research Log

51.198 N · 9.421 E · nr. Gudensberg · ~40 km S · compiled 21 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Tier-3 “Deissen” place-name candidate (James’s reading of “Deisse”). Full baptism-window sweep completed 22 June 2026: the entire 1834–1841 Taufen run of register 253403 (Seiten 8–31, ~110 baptisms) was read leaf-by-leaf in the Archion viewer — not one male child named Carl/Karl appears. No Konze pool. Negative.

Confirmation-register sweep — “any Carl, 1833–1841”

Part of the June 2026 widened sweep across the Kreis Hofgeismar — looking for any boy named Carl/Karl born 1833–1841, surname open. Dissen kept no separate confirmation book for the window (its confirmands were registered at Gudensberg), so the sweep was run directly against the baptism register instead: Dissen Taufen 1830–1924, Archion reg. 253403 ↗ (Kirchenkreis Fritzlar). The whole 1834–1841 window was read entry-by-entry. The cross-parish ledger and method/reliability notes live on Parish search coverage.

All eight year-leaves were read directly off the Archion images at high zoom; the per-image permalinks above were minted 22 June 2026 and are the citation anchors. Two male entries (1839 Nro 103, 1841 Nro 142) are recorded only as “[sex], unnamed” (stillborn pattern) and carry no legible given name — neither is a Carl, and neither could be Charles (who lived to 1903). The register’s body renders the “Jahr” header and running Nro consistently; the image-number map above is calibrated against those headers and the year-end tallies.
