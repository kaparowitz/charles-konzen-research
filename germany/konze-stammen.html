Stammen Konze documented · swineherd family fully reconstructed

Solved (Session 96): the Stammen swineherd Andreas Kontze is a son of Arnold Philipp Konze of Trendelburg (his 23 Mar 1783 marriage names the father) — resolving the 1787 “Halbbruder” entry: he and Joh. Henrich the younger were half-brothers. The couple had twelve children (1784–1809), both parents died in Stammen (1817/1828), and Christian Ludwig was baptized HERE on 23 Nov 1806 — the 1833 “gebürtig von Gottsbüren” statement is contradicted by his own baptism entry. Update (Session 97): the marriage book confirms the swineherd remarried in 1818 as a widower (“Pfundmeister”); and Christian Ludwig’s couple did not leave Stammen after 1835 — Anne Gertrud died at Haus Nr. 21 in 1869 with him still her living husband. The two untraced sons (Johann Christian b.1785, Johann Henrich b.1800) did NOT marry in Stammen 1789–1831.
