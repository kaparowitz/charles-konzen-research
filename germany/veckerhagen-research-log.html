Veckerhagen — Parish Research Log

51.493 N · 9.593 E · reg. 220213 · ~14.7 km SE of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Jan–Mar 1835 baptisms searched. One illegitimate male — Wilhelm Walter, baptized ~March 1835 (mother Anna Martha Johanne Schäfer) — outside the window and unrelated; not a match. No Konze-pool surname.

Confirmation register sweep COMPLETED (26 Jun 2026) — NEGATIVE. Veckerhagen keeps its own Konfirmationen book (Konfirmationen 1831–1901, reg. 220207), so a confirmation sweep applies. The full window 1847–1855 (births ~1833–1841) has now been read — the prime 1849 class (Charles's exact 1835-birth cohort) and 1850 in full, plus every flanking year 1847, 1848, 1851, 1852, 1853, 1854, 1855. No confirmand in any year carries a Konze / Gannsen / *n-s-n / *n-z-n surname. The forename Carl/Karl recurs in every class but always on a local Veckerhagen surname: Carl Ackermann & Carl Geith (1847); Carl Braun (b. 21 Jun 1835) & Carl Louis Roth/Koch (1849); Carl Dettmar & Carl Wiegand (1850); Carl Dettmar, Carl Gunkel & Carl Bartheld (1851); Carl Schmidt & Carl Becker (1852); Carl Brunzland (1853); Carl Dallmann (1855); plus several middle-name Carls. No boy born 26 Jan 1835 of any surname — the closest 1835 Carls are Braun (21 Jun 1835) and Dittmar (12 Feb 1835, per the 25 Jun read), both ruled out by surname. Calibration note: the 24 Jun "PARTIAL" entry below mis-dated the 1835/1836 classes as "1855/1856" (the "Carl Anton Sahl b.~1834" was from the 1835 class, out-of-window) — corrected 25–26 Jun. The reel is essentially linear (Bild 6 = Seite 1–2 = 1831, ~2 images/yr) with duplicate-exposure pairs (44≡45, 55≡56) and two displaced leaves (Seite 80–81 = 1847 rows 13+; Seite 102–103 = 1853 rows 18–35) — both off-cohort years, optional residual. Verdict: well-saturated NEGATIVE for Charles's confirmation.
