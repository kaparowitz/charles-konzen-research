Vernawahlshausen — Parish Research Log

Weser valley, Kreis Hofgeismar · N of Gieselwerder · Taufen reg. 220261 · Konf. reg. 220258 · compiled 26 June 2026
⛔ ELIMINATED — negative on BOTH the baptism and confirmation tracks

A Konze-pool parish from Klaus Kunze’s Ortssippenbuch, here attesting the surname Gans only. Vernawahlshausen sits in the Weser valley north of Gieselwerder, in the old Kreis Hofgeismar. Both record tracks were closed out live in the logged-in Archion viewer on 26 June 2026: the alphabetical baptism index (1830–1869) and the confirmation register (1831–1948).

Baptism index — clean negative. The reel carries a legible end-of-reel alphabetical name index (AI-transcribable). The full G section (Bild 1564) holds only Gebrunner, Goerder/Görder and Giebert — no Gans / Ganß / Ganson anywhere — and the full K section (Bild 1567) holds only Kummel, Klinge, Küchemann, Koch — no Konze / Kontze / Jungkonze. The OSB-attested “Gans” does not appear in the baptism index at all.

Confirmations — negative (newly searched). Read by eye across classes 1847–1856 (Bild 1152–1163), including both prime classes for Charles’s cohort. The 1849 class (Bild 1157) had only two boys — Pfütz (b. 20 Nov 1834) and Funke (b. 26 March 1835); the 1850 class’s lone Carl was Carl Wilhelm Wißmann (b. 1835). No Gans/Konze confirmand, and no boy named Carl/Karl on a pool surname. The nearest near-miss (Carl Wilhelm Wißmann) is the wrong surname and the wrong date.

Bottom line. No Carl/Karl b. 26 Jan 1835 on a Konze/Gans surname; the pool surname “Gans” is absent from both registers entirely. Confirmations move “Not yet” → Swept (negative); the baptism index moves “effectively negative (sampled)” → clean negative (index fully read). Vernawahlshausen is eliminated.

Baptism index — Taufen 1830–1869 (reg. 220261)

The reel is “mit Register”: a legible alphabetical name-index sits at the end of the reel (≈ Bild 1560–1567), two letters per opening (columns Männlich | Seite | Weiblich | Seite per letter). On a clean redraw the index reads in plain alphabetical order and the viewer’s AI “Erkannter Text” panel transcribes the leaves reliably. Letter map: 1560 = B|C, 1561 = C|D, 1563 = E|F, 1564 = F|G, … 1567 = K. Baptism leaves calibrate Seite = image − 1296.

Classes 1845–46 (births ~1830–32) are out of range for an 1835 boy and not individually read; 1854 covered via the 1853/1855 brackets.

Verdict

⛔ Eliminated — Charles is not here

Across the baptism index (1830–1869) and the confirmation classes 1847–1856, there is no Carl/Karl born 26 Jan 1835 on a Konze/Gans surname, and the OSB-attested pool surname “Gans” does not appear at all in either register. Vernawahlshausen is closed as a candidate parish for Charles Gannsen.

Remaining follow-ups

  • None — parish eliminated. Both tracks fully read and negative; no open leads remain here. Detailed evidence: Session 127 log (Confirmations + Baptism-Index, 26 Jun 2026).
