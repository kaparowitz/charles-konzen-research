Marzhausen — Parish Research Log

51.376 N · 9.918 E · Taufen reg. 181898 · Konfirmationen reg. 181895 · Kirchenkreis Witzenhausen · ~40 km SE · compiled 21 June 2026, confirmation sweep 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Checked for completeness (neighbouring Kirchenkreis). 1835 baptisms all married couples; no Konze/Köster, no illegitimate male.

Confirmation register now swept (24 June 2026). Every Marzhausen confirmation class from 1846 through 1856 read leaf-by-leaf (reg. 181895), fully bracketing the 1833–1841 birth window — negative. No boy confirmed under the rufname Carl/Karl with a Konze/Kontze/Gansen or other *n·s·n surname. The surname pool is a tight local Witzenhausen-district set (Hafer, Grimme, Löhner, Apel, Decker, Ullrich, Niedermann, Albrecht, Leube, Lambach, Günther, Diedrich, Bischof, Maaß, Uttermöhlen). The prime 1849 class (the exact cohort for a boy b. 26 Jan 1835) held only two boys — Lambach (b. 1834) and Andreas Wilhelm Adolf Wilhelm (b. 1835) — neither a Carl.
