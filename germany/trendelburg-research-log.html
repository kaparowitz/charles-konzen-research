Trendelburg — Parish Research Log

51.584 N · 9.420 E · ~1.8 km north of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026
WORKING HYPOTHESIS

The parish complex where the Konze / Kontzen / Contzen surname is most densely documented. Register 220108 (Taufen 1830–1863) alphabetical index lists “Konze, Johann Friedrich Wilhelm — Seite 243”; the load-bearing surname register 220096 (KB 1773–1818) carries Kontze baptisms, marriages and burials read entry-by-entry.

June 2026 FamilySearch-tree reconciliation surfaced a same-named Trendelburg son, Carl Wilhelm Konze (FS 99Z3-DFK, b. 15 Sep 1831, son of Ludwig Karl Konze 1804 × Wilhelmine Marie Albrecht; first cousin of Justus Ernst) — then eliminated him: he stayed, married Marie Lauterbach, and died at Trendelburg 30 Oct 1869.

Confirmation-register sweep — “any Carl, 1833–1841”

Part of the June 2026 widened confirmation-register sweep across the Kreis Hofgeismar — looking for any boy named Carl/Karl born 1833–1841, surname open, who would surface in the 1847–1855 confirmation classes. This is the parish-specific row; the cross-parish ledger, the full list of boys named Carl that turned up, and the method/reliability notes live on Parish search coverage.

Follow-ups resolved (21 June 2026)

Closed — negative for Charles

Full 1833–41 baptism re-scan of 220108. Read entry-by-entry across 1833 (Nr.58–90), 1834 (Nr.91–~117) and Jan–Apr 1835 (Nr.118–135) — including the prime window around 26 Jan 1835 — then cross-checked against the register’s alphabetical K-surname index. No Konze (or Kontze/Contze) boy was baptised anywhere in 1833–1841. The only Konze-surname baptism in the span is Christine Wilh. Phil. Konze (female, b.1 Aug 1834, Seite 31, daughter of Carl Ludwig Konze × Wilhelmine Albrecht) — that couple’s daughter, which precludes a son born 26 Jan 1835. The "Carl ~Jan 1835" baptisms are Carl Wilhelm Henze (S.37 №123 — the already-resolved "Ganzen" lead) and Joh. Carl August Graff (S.38 №124), both non-Konze.

Confirmation classes 1833–1841 (220102), per Jed’s request. Every class read. These cohorts (born ~1818–1827) are too old to be Charles, and none is a Konze boy of any age matching him. Five genuine Konze/Jungkonze confirmands surfaced — all enrich the family tree (≥3 Jungkonze households: Johannes; Johann George; Ludwig × Meerkamm — plus a Konze daughter Sophie and the Loenges × Konze line), none is Charles.

~38 resident Konze with missing vitals (blind scan). Effectively closed by the alphabetical surname index, which enumerates every Konze/Kontze baptism with its Seite; no in-window male baptism exists beyond those already eliminated. The burial-index route was verified dead 13 June.

1847–1850 confirmation classes (220102) — the previously queued item, now read live (22 June 2026). These are the classes that could actually contain Charles (b.26 Jan 1835 → confirmed at age ~14–15 in 1849 or 1850), unlike the 1833–41 classes above. Every class 1847–1850 was read entry-by-entry directly in the viewer. No Konze / Kontze / Gannsen / Jungkonze surname appears among any readable confirmand, and none is recorded born 26 Jan 1835. The born-1835 boys present are all non-Konze (1849: Lotze, Biermann, Röse, Grothein; 1850: Albrecht, Korte). One honest gap remains: the 1849 class’s first nine confirmands (all boys, Seite 84/85) are on a leaf not filmed in this digitisation — in its place the register has a duplicated 1850 page (image 2270, duplicated again at 2273). A Konze among those nine is not excluded, but no Konze boy was baptised in Trendelburg 1833–41 and none appears among the seventeen readable 1849 names.
