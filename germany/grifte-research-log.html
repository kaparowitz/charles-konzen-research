Grifte — Parish Research Log

51.223 N · 9.426 E · Edermünde · ~37 km S · compiled 21 June 2026 · full 1833–1841 baptism sweep 22 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Tier-3 “Deissen” place-name candidate. The full 1833–1841 baptism window (Archion reg. 253444) was read leaf-by-leaf on 22 June 2026 — ~157 baptisms, entries 36–192 — and is negative: no boy carries Carl as a first / rufname, no Konze or *n·s·n surname appears anywhere, and there is no 26 January 1835 birth (1835’s first birth is mid-February). The single incidental “Carl” in the whole window is a third given name (1834, entry 73) on a local non-matching surname. No Konze pool.

Baptism-register sweep — “any Carl, 1833–1841” (full window, with permalinks)

Part of the June 2026 widened sweep across the Kreis Hofgeismar — looking for any boy named Carl/Karl born 1833–1841, surname open. Grifte kept no confirmation book for the window (its confirmations were held at Gudensberg), so the sweep reads the baptism register directly instead of an 1847–55 confirmation class. Reg. 253444 ↗ (Grifte Taufen 1830–1883, “Amt Gudensberg”) was walked leaf-by-leaf on 22 June 2026; every child-name column 1833–1841 was read by eye. The register’s leaves are partly out of Seite-order and carry duplicate scans, so image numbers were de-duplicated against the printed Jahr/Seite header on each leaf; each year below carries a one-click Archion permalink to its anchor leaf. The cross-parish ledger and method/reliability notes live on Parish search coverage.

Confirmation-book check (24 June 2026): Grifte’s Archion holdings were inspected directly — the parish lists only Beerdigungen (1830–1908, 1909–1983), the combined Kirchenbuch volumes (1649–1703, 1703–1738, 1739–1814, 1773–1841), a Nebenkirchenbuch 1857–1876, Taufen (1830–1883, 1884–1925), and Trauungen (1830–1911, 1912–1955). There is no separate Konfirmationen register: the combined church-books end in 1841 and the Nebenkirchenbuch begins in 1857, so nothing in Grifte’s own holdings touches the 1847–1855 confirmation window. This confirms the “no confirmation book” label — its confirmands were registered at Gudensberg — and the baptism register is the correct surrogate.

Window already aligned (24 June 2026): the window-edge was spot-checked on Archion. Unlike Dissen (whose baptism sweep had started only at 1834 and needed a 1830–1833 extension), Grifte’s sweep already opens at the true start of January 1833 (Nro 36, Seite 10, img 974) and runs through 1841, so the full confirmation-equivalent window of births (~1833–1841) was already covered end-to-end. No extension was needed. Births before 1833 (Nro 1–35, 1830–1832) fall outside the window and outside Charles’s 1835 birth year, so they were not read.

Reading caveat (per the project standard): the given name Carl is reliably distinguishable from Conrad/Johann in Kurrent, so the “is there a Carl?” result is sound; exact surnames and birth-day numerals are best readings from the by-eye pass and warrant a Kurrent specialist’s second eye. Entry 73’s father-surname in particular is worth confirming, though it is plainly not a Konze / *n·s·n form.
