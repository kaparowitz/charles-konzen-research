Lamerden — Parish Research Log

51.610 N · 9.392 E · Taufen reg. 219676 · Konfirmationen reg. 219673 · ~5.0 km NW of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026, updated 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Searched on both tracks and negative on both. Baptism: Jan–Feb 1835 read by eye (register not AI-transcribed) — no Konze-pool male, no illegitimate male in the window. Confirmation: the full 1846–1855 classes (every confirmand born 1833–1841) read by eye in reg. 219673 — the forename Carl recurs ~15× but never on a Konze / *n-s-n / *n-z-n surname, and no Konze-type surname appears in any class.

Baptism-register index check — K / I-J / G (26 June 2026)

The alphabetical name-index of the Taufen register (reg. 219676) was read for the in-pool surnames — negative. K (Bild 451–452, a large two-page block) = Plate, Klage, Klö-, Krote, Rückert in the legible entries — no Konze/Kontze. G (Bild 448) = near-empty — no Gans/Ganson. The index pages are partly shuffled and the K block is dense in a difficult Kurrent hand, so the K read is best-effort-by-eye (certify via Peter Schräder if ever needed); the comprehensive Lamerden confirmation sweep (219673, 1846–1855, all classes) already covers the birth window and is likewise negative. Detail: Session125 log.
