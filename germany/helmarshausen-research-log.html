Helmarshausen — Parish Research Log

51.605 N · 9.491 E · ~6.5 km NE of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmation sweep completed (1847–1855, all leaves) 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Full-index search of the Helmarshausen KB returned no Konze-pool male born ~1835. Eliminated April 2026. The June 2026 confirmation-register sweep (reg. 219208, 1848–1855 classes) corroborates: several boys named Carl, none on a Konze / *n-s-n surname.

Confirmation-register sweep — “any Carl, 1833–1841”

Part of the June 2026 widened confirmation-register sweep across the Kreis Hofgeismar — looking for any boy named Carl/Karl born 1833–1841, surname open, who would surface in the 1847–1855 confirmation classes. This is the parish-specific row; the cross-parish ledger, the full list of boys named Carl that turned up, and the method/reliability notes live on Parish search coverage.

The three residual jumble-blocked leaves were located and read live 24 June 2026 — all negative: the 1847 class (S.85–86, img 1289; 12 confirmands; Carls = Franz Carl Heinrich Jüstel #5 b.1834, Carl Heinrich Grimmer #11 b.1834; p/258c511a31); the 1849 boys 1–10 (S.91–92, img 1292; Carls = Johann Carl Nübern #1, Hermann…Carl Wagner #5, both b.1834; p/3070969599); and the 1850 boys 1–14 (S.97–100, imgs 1295–1296; Carls = Carl Wilhelm Theis #1, b. 30 May 1836 — the prime-cohort boy, surname Theis, Carl Julius Bringemann #9, Johann Carl Ludwig Vogt #12, Carl Friedrich Wilhelm Apel #14; boys-1–5 leaf p/282ec58033). No Konze / *n-s-n surname on any Carl in any leaf.
