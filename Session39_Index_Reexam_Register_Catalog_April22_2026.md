## Analysis

### The Index Error Pattern

Two of the eight Konze-connected index entries (380 and 461) have confirmed wrong entry numbers. This is a 25% error rate for the K section index, which is high. Possible explanations:

**A. Transposition errors**: The index clerk may have swapped digits when copying entry numbers. For example:
- 380 could be a swap of 308, 830, or 803
- 461 could be a swap of 416, 641, or 146

**B. Misread source**: The clerk may have been working from a rough draft or earlier listing and copied numbers incorrectly.

**C. The remaining unchecked entries (311, 328, ~361, 406) may also have errors.** Only entries 358, 359, and 379 have been confirmed as accurate mappings.

### The Wilhelmine Konze Mystery

The index lists TWO entries that could refer to Wilhelmine née Konze:
1. "Konze, Wilhelmine 461" — listed under maiden name, wrong entry number
2. "Köster, Wilhelmine geb. Konze 406" — listed under married name

If these refer to the SAME person, then "Wilhelmine Konze" was actually "Wilhelmine Köster née Konze" — a Konze daughter who married a Köster. Her actual entry number would be 406 (if that mapping is correct). This person would NOT be Wilhelmine geb. Hildebrand (wife of J.H. Konze #2) because her maiden name is Konze, not Hildebrand.

If they are DIFFERENT people:
- "Konze, Wilhelmine 461" could be Wilhelmine Konze née Hildebrand (wife of J.H. Konze #2 who kept her married surname "Konze")
- "Köster, Wilhelmine geb. Konze 406" is a different Wilhelmine who was born Konze and married a Köster

### The Hildebrand Connection

Entry 468 (Image 1869) references "Förster Hildebrand" — suggesting the Hildebrand family included a forester (Förster) in Deisel. This is relevant because:
- Wilhelmine Hildebrand (wife of J.H. Konze #2) could be from this forester family
- A Förster was a government-appointed position with social standing
- This could explain why the Konze × Hildebrand marriage was not found in Deisel Trauungen — the marriage may have taken place in the Hildebrand family's home parish, which could be different from Deisel if the Förster served a wider district

### Expanded Konze Family Network

The Konze daughters married into at least FIVE different Deisel families:
1. **Köster** — Maria Elisabeth geb. Konze (Images 9, 22); Wilhelmine geb. Konze (Index 406); Conrad Konze's wife Anna Catharina was previously Köster
2. **Kuhlmann** — Anna Eufrosine geb. Konze (Index ~361)
3. **Sasse** — Amelia geb. Konze (Entry 465), married clockmaker Johannes Sasse
4. **Jordan** — Catharina Elisabeth Jordan married Johannes Konze, Ackermann (Entry 379)
5. **Hildebrand** — Wilhelmine Hildebrand married J.H. Konze #2 (Entry 195 baptism)
