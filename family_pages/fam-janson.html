15. Joh. Heinrich Janson line — Janson family · Hofgeismar (adjacent Deisel)

Latest 3 December 2025 · "the Jansons updated" · status: eliminated

Peter's genealogical content

  • Johann Heinrich Janson b. 14 Aug 1792, son of Joh. Georg Janson × Anna Catharina Müller; d. 27 Feb 1849.
  • m. 21 Nov 1830 Hofgeismar to Wilhelmine Luc b. 3 Apr 1808 Hofgeismar-Friedrichsdorf, daughter of Frédéric Luc × Marie Meyer.
  • Children: stillborn male (1.12.1831); Gertrud Friederike Amalie (b. 1832); Marie Louise Caroline (b. 1834); Joh. Friedrich Ludwig (b. 1837); Elise Sabine Albertine (b. 1840); David (b./d. 1843).
  • Maria Janson — emigrated 1861 via Bremen, possibly to U.S.

Why this line was rejected

After Janson the father died 1849, the widow Wilhelmine and four surviving children disappear from Hofgeismar registers. None of the boys' birth years align with Charles's ~1830s birth window. No U.S. match was confirmed. Quietly set aside.
