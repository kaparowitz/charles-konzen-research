5. Carl Ludwig Lucas line — Lucas family, Trendelburg · February 1835

Latest update 13 April 2026 · "Re: new candidate" · status: eliminated

Peter's genealogical content

  • Carl Ludwig Lucas — born Trendelburg February 1835, illegitimate, father unknown.
  • Confirmation 1849 in Trendelburg.
  • Emigrated March 1862 — official destination given as "Prussia," no further details.
  • Timing fits Charles: leaves Germany early enough to potentially join the U.S. Army December 1862.

Why this line was rejected

Jed found a German marriage record for Carl Ludwig Lucas. Peter confirmed 13 April 2026: "without any doubts — he is the man from Trendelburg. Another dead end." He stayed in Germany / went to Prussia, never crossed the Atlantic. (The specific marriage register and entry are not reproduced on this site; the elimination rests on Peter Schräder's identification of the record.) Peter then ordered presbyterial protocols from the Landeskirchliches Archiv in Kassel for further illegitimate-birth interrogations.
