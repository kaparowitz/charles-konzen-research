11. Maria Sophia Hofeditz × Andreas Konze line — Hofeditz family, Konze in-laws · Trendelburg / Deisel

5–7 April 2026 · "Andreas Konze" thread · status: eliminated as direct line
Genealogical content
  • Maria Sophia Hofeditz × Andreas Konze (b. 1795).
  • Father (Andreas) "lost without a trace" — recorded in baptism marginalia.
  • Their son Justus Ernst Konze b. 14 Nov 1829 — last seen Pfingsten 1843 confirmation in Deisel.
  • Earlier Wilhelmina Hofedietz × Andreas Kontze (b. 1787) — the Trendelburg side, marriage 8 Mar 1807.
Why this line was rejected as direct

Eliminated as Charles's direct line — descends from a different Andreas Konze than the Entry 195 candidate. Retained as collateral evidence for the Konze family's Deisel/Trendelburg residence.
