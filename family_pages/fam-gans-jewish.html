8. Salomon Gans / Segelbaum line — Meimbressen Jewish family

Latest attachment 24 March 2026 · "Meimbressen" · status: eliminated
Peter's genealogical content
  • Jewish Gans family of Meimbressen in the Hofgeismar Kirchenkreis.
  • Forced under Hessian Jewish-naming law to change surname to Segelbaum.
  • Family emigrated 1853/54 to the U.S.; German emigration paperwork is confused on the group.
  • Son Salomon Gans Americanized his given name to Charles on arrival — false-positive on first name.
  • Surname-resemblance theory: "Gans-son" → "Gansson" → "Gannsen."
Why this line was rejected

Jed traced the family's U.S. arrival: Salomon Gans is a real Charles, but the wrong family, wrong age, wrong destination. Eliminated 25 March 2026.
