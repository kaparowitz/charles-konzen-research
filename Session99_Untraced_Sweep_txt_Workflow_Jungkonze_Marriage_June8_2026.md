## B. Archion txt/HTR — how it actually works (verified)
- The viewer **txt** button opens a panel; on an un-recognized page it offers **"Order text recognition / Texterkennung beauftragen."** Clicking **spends 1 credit** and **queues** the page. Result is **asynchronous** — appears in the panel after *several minutes* (both pages I queued were still "coming soon" at session end; ARCHION-reserved images take longer).
- With 0 credits the panel says "Kontingent fehlt" (no free reads); after Jed's top-up the Order button appears. **Not instant, not free-per-view** → use for targeted pages only.
- **No alphabetical index in Stammen.** 220063 title page: "(1775–1831) **ohne Register**." 220072 (Beerdigungen 1830–1936) runs entries straight from the title page with no Namensregister at either end. ⇒ Session 97's "Stammen K-index" was **not** a register index; there is no index shortcut. Need a known year or page anchor.
- **Free vs paid:** visual screenshot/zoom navigation is free; only the txt transcription costs a credit. Efficient pattern = find the page visually, then txt it.
