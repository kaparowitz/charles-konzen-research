Immenhausen — Parish Research Log

51.426 N · 9.478 E · Taufen reg. 219586 · Konfirmationen reg. 219580 · ~16.3 km south of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmation sweep 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

1835 baptisms searched: all males legitimate with named fathers; no Konze-pool surname, no illegitimate male.

Confirmation sweep (24 June 2026): the Konfirmationen 1831–1919 (reg. 219580) were read class-by-class across the full 1847–1855 window (births ~1833–1841), the cohort in which a boy born 26 Jan 1835 would have been confirmed. The forename Carl recurs about a dozen times but never on a Konze / Kontze / Gansen / Gonsen / *n-s-n* surname, and no boy was born 26 Jan 1835. The prime 1849 and 1850 classes — the exact cohort for the working birthdate — hold five Carls between them (Engelhard, Jäger, Homburg, Friedrich Carl, Waldeck), all on local non-matching surnames. Immenhausen is now searched on both the baptism (1835) and confirmation (1847–1855) tracks — negative on both.

Baptism name-index check — C / G / I·J / K (reg. 219586)
