Ostheim — Parish Research Log

51.529 N · 9.300 E · reg. 219970 (Taufen) · Konf. reg. 219967 · ~9.2 km WSW of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmation sweep 25 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Jan–Feb 1835: one stillborn male (unnamed, unbaptized — not a candidate by definition) and legitimate males only; no Konze-pool surname, no illegitimate male.

Both tracks negative. Confirmation sweep (reg. 219967, prime 1849 & 1850 classes read in full): the one prime-cohort Carl is Karl Gänßler (b. ~1834, father Georg Christian Gänßler, a local Leinweber) — a phonetic near-miss on the root “Gäns-” but ending “-ßler,” not the canon *n·s·n*, and plainly a local weaver family, not Konze. The 1849 class also holds George Ludwig Lucke (b. 24 Jan 1835 — two days off the anchor, but not a Carl). No boy born 26 Jan 1835.
