Grebenstein — Parish Research Log

51.447 N · 9.411 E · reg. 219106 · ~13.5 km south of Deisel · compiled 21 June 2026 · confirmations 1847–1851 swept 24 June 2026
ELIMINATED

Jan–Feb 1835 baptisms searched: one illegitimate January birth but female; no Konze-pool male.

Confirmation register now also swept on the prime cohort (24 Jun 2026): the Konfirmationen (reg. 219097, well-ordered, Seite = 2×Bild − 3656) classes 1848, 1849 and 1850 were read confirmand-by-confirmand in the Archion viewer. Charles's exact 1849 class (births ~1834–35, 28 boys) contains no Carl at all; the three Carls across 1848–1850 are Carl Junge (1848, illeg.), Carl Adolph Studt (1848) and Carl Lahrenbach (1850, b. 1836) — none on a Konze / *n-s-n surname.

Sweep extended (24 Jun 2026, later run): the rest of the probative window was read — the 1850 tail (boys 15–28 incl. Karl Wilhelm [Karl], b. 1836; girls 29–42) and the full 1851 class (entries 1–48: Carls Limberger, Rosenhauer, Schmidt, Schäfer — all local surnames) plus the 1847 class entries 5–37 (incl. Carl Z[eilenbrand], b. 3 Feb 1833). No Konze / *n-s-n surname in any class. The window 1847–1851 (births 1832–1837, spanning Charles's anchor) is now decisively negative. Residual completeness tail left for a follow-up run: 1852–1855 (births 1838–1841, Bild ~1911–1920) and 1847 head-entries 1–4 (Bild 1896) — the youngest window years, where Charles (b. 1835) cannot appear.
