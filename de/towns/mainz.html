Historical overview

Mainz, the Rheinhessen city on the Rhine, is far south of the Deisel cluster but enters the investigation as the place where the documented Friedrichsfeld Kontze branch settled — Andreas Kontze (b. 1 Oct 1795 Trendelburg) died here in 1854.

Significance to the investigation

Checked as the Konze family’s southern settlement, following Andreas Kontze’s line. There is no Charles-line male birth ~1835 here; the branch settled south only after the 1840s. The Mainz trail documents where the family went, not where Charles was born. Eliminated as a birth parish (family trail).
