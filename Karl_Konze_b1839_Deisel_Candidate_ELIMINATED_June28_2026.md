# Candidate ELIMINATED — Karl (Carl) Konze, b. 7 June 1839, Deisel

**Date logged:** 28 June 2026
**Status:** ❌ **ELIMINATED** as a candidate for Charles Gannsen
**Candidate proposed by:** Klaus Kunze (email, 28 June 2026)
**Elimination basis:** Ancestry **Hesse, Germany, Deaths, 1851–1958** record (death age 81,
b. ~1839, d. 23 Sept 1920 Homberg/Efze), which uniquely matches and corroborates Peter
Schräder's Hessian genealogical database (email, 28 Aug 2025).

> **Provenance note:** I searched the project for a saved "AncestryPro" research file and
> found none (only generic references to a 2020 AncestryPro Deisel baptism search). The
> elimination rests on two mutually-corroborating sources: the **Peter Schräder "Deisel"
> email thread (Aug 2025)** and the **Ancestry Hesse death record** found 28 Jun 2026
> (link below). Note on the marriage: Ancestry's *Hesse, Germany, Marriages, 1849–1930*
> index returns several different "Karl Konze" men in scattered towns (Haiger 1907, Hebel
> 1903, etc.) with **no birth date in the index**, so none can be tied to *this* Karl with
> confidence — the death record is the clean, unique identifier and is what is cited here.

## The candidate (as identified by Klaus Kunze, 28 June 2026)

Kunze reviewed the Deisel Taufregister (Archion reg. 218842) from 1835 onward at Jed's request
and reported:

> "Ich habe die Taufregister ab 1835 durchgesehen. Der Name Gans kommt nicht vor. Ein Ehepaar
> Konze ließ seit 1835 mehrere Kinder taufen, es hatte auch eine Totgeburt. **Am 7.6.1839 wurde
> dem Paar ein Sohn Karl geboren. Das könnte er sein.** Vater Johann Christoph Konze V., Pate des
> Vaters Bruder Gottlieb Konze, Schullehrer in Langental."

Candidate particulars:

Kunze also noted the **absence pattern** that first made Karl look promising:

> "Im alphabetischen Sterberegister steht kein Tod von Karl Konze. Im Konfirmandenregister steht
> keine Konfirmation um 1853. Man sollte daraus schließen, daß Karl nach dem Tod seines Vaters
> Deisel verlassen hat."

→ No Deisel death entry and no Deisel confirmation (~1853) for Karl ⇒ he left Deisel after his
father's death (1845). On its own this is an *emigration-shaped* gap, which is why Kunze flagged
him as "could be him."

## Why he is NOT Charles — the elimination

### 1. He married and stayed in Germany; died at Homberg an der Efze in 1920

The "left Deisel" gap is **not** an emigration — Karl simply moved within Hesse. Peter Schräder's
database resolves the gap directly. In the **"Deisel" email thread, 28 Aug 2025**, Peter wrote:

> "the only person with the same name I could find in Deisel was Karl Konze, but **he died in
> Homberg/Efze 1920.** There is only 1 person with the family-name Konze in Deisel with no further
> information: Phillip Konze, born November 1841, confirmation 1855 in Deisel."

This is confirmed directly by the **Ancestry Hesse death record** (found 28 Jun 2026):

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Name | Karl Konze |
| Death age | **81** (⇒ born ~1839) |
| Birth date | abt 1839 |
| Death date | **23 Sept 1920** |
| Death place | **Homberg Efze (Homberg), Hessen, Germany** |
| Civil registration office | Homberg (Efze) · Certificate Nr. 45 |
| Collection | Ancestry, *Hesse, Germany, Deaths, 1851–1958* |
| Citation | Hessisches Hauptstaatsarchiv, Wiesbaden; Sterberegister Bestand 3597, Lfd. Nr. 920 |
| **Record URL** | https://www.ancestry.com/search/collections/61119/records/383240 |
| Image URL | https://www.ancestry.com/imageviewer/collections/61119/images/homberg-efze-_3597_1918-1922-00164?pid=383240 |

A man who lived to **die in Homberg an der Efze on 23 Sept 1920 at age 81** remained in Germany
his whole life. He therefore **cannot** be Charles, who died in **St. Louis, Missouri in 1903**.
The two life-paths are mutually exclusive.

### 2. The birth dates do not match

Charles's American records put his birth at **26 January 1835** (with at most a year or two of
slack). Karl Konze was born **7 June 1839** — roughly **4½ years off**, outside the plausible
window even before the geographic contradiction.

In the same Aug 2025 thread Peter also checked Charles's stated date directly:

> "Had also a look at the births in Deisel 26.1.1835 in Deisel, this dat was mentioned in Carls
> death record - nothing."

→ No Deisel birth on 26 Jan 1835 at all; the only Deisel Karl Konze (the 1839 boy) is the one
who lived and died in Germany.

---

## Verdict

The Karl/Carl Konze born 7 June 1839 in Deisel — the candidate Klaus Kunze raised on 28 June
2026 — is **eliminated** on two independent grounds: (a) he married and remained in Germany,
dying at Homberg an der Efze in 1920 (Schräder database + Jed's Ancestry Pro Tools), and
(b) his 1839 birth is ~4½ years off Charles's recorded 26 Jan 1835. The "vanished from Deisel"
appearance that made him look like an emigrant is explained by an intra-German move, not
emigration.

This does **not** disturb the broader Deisel/Konze hypothesis; it removes one specific individual
from contention and confirms Kunze's own closing inference (no Deisel 26 Jan 1835 birth).
