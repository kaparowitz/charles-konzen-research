## Headline

Session 49 performed a targeted page-by-page scan of ALL burial entries spanning late 1834 through early 1836 in Register 218851 (Deisel Beerdigungen 1830–1876), covering entries 138–164 across Images 1812–1816. **Critical result: NO infant Konze death was found.** The Entry 195 child (male, born night of Jan 23–24, 1835; father Joh. Heinrich Konze, Kaufmann; mother Wilhelmine geb. Hildebrand) has no death record anywhere in the Deisel burial register. This is now a **triple-confirmed negative** — (1) no death cross in baptism notes, (2) no matching entry in the 1830–1876 alphabetical burial index (Sessions 37–39), (3) no entry in the page-by-page scan of 1835 burials. **The Entry 195 child survived infancy and left Deisel — making this child the strongest single-record candidate for Charles Gannsen.**

Additionally, one significant Konze burial was found: **Entry 152 — Johannes Konze** (b. 1810, d. 1835), lediger Sohn des Andreas Konze, at No. 29. Two "geb. Konze" references were also found (Entries 155 and 157).
