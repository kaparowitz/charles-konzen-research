# Session 122 — Village Confirmation Sweeps: Hombressen, Haueda, Ostheim, Niedermeiser

**Date:** 25 June 2026
**Task:** Extend the widened "any Carl/Karl, born 1833–1841, surname open" confirmation
sweep (run for Immenhausen in Session 120) to the four remaining Kreis-Hofgeismar
parishes still flagged **"confirmation classes not yet read"** on `search-coverage`.
All four had previously been cleared only on their 1835 baptism windows.

## Method (same as Session 120)

For each parish: locate the *Konfirmationen* register via the Archion parish
book-list, open the viewer, calibrate the image↔year mapping, read the **prime
1849–1850 classes** (the cohort for the working anchor **26 Jan 1835**) in full,
scan the flanking 1847–1855 years at forename level, and mint a permalink for the
prime class. Every boy's given name was checked for **Carl/Karl**; the surname of
every Carl was checked against the Konze / Kontze / Gansen / Gonsen / *n·s·n*
canon. Surname reads are by-eye Kurrent, to be certified by Peter Schräder.
