### Interpretation

The Deisel Registerband K-section definitively confirms a continuous Kontze family line in Deisel from at least the 1717 birth of Andreas Kontze through the 1817 death of a Joh[ann] Kontze son of Andreas. The 2 Jan 1717 Andreas aligns with — and refines — the research-tree estimate of "Andreas Kontze (I) Küster, b. ~1718." The 1773 burial date conflicts with the research-tree estimate of "d. 1791" and requires reconciliation (one possibility: two distinct Andreases, father d.1773 and son or collateral d.1791).

The 14 Sep 1748 Andreas (entry 114) is a candidate for Andreas Kontze (I)'s son; the 11 Jan 1740 Joh. (entry 117) is a candidate for an elder brother or half-brother, with two marriages including to a "Kontze Maria" (endogamous within the family) and a long life ending 1817.

His son Justus Ernst b. 14 Nov 1829 and any hypothesized later son Carl b. 26 Jan 1835 are in the successor volumes 218824 (1796–1831) and its 1831+ follow-on.
