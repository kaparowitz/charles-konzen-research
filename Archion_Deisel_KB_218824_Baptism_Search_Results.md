### 3.3 Godparents (Gevatternin)

1. **Anna Catharina, wife of Johann Henrich Kontzen** — This is the widow of Johann Henrich Kontze of Deisel (b. ~1747, d. 17 April 1792). She was née Köster (per the Family Tree document). Her presence as godparent directly connects Klaus Andreas Kontze to the established Deisel Kontze family.
2. **Anna Elisabeth, wife of Dragoner David Lauterbach** — David Lauterbach was a Dragoon (cavalry soldier) in Deisel. His own son "Johann Henrich" was baptized in the 1798 section (image 1657).
3. **Anna Margareta née Koch** — From the Koch family of Deisel, another well-established local family.

### 3.4 Critical Observation: The Schildknecht → Kontze Surname Correction

The most striking feature of this entry is the **surname correction**. The pastor initially wrote the father's surname as **"Schildknecht"** and then crossed it out and wrote **"Kontze"** above or beside it. This suggests:

- Klaus Andreas may have been known by **both surnames** in the community
- He may have been a **Schildknecht by upbringing** (stepchild, foster child, or illegitimate child raised by a Schildknecht family) who was actually a **biological Kontze**
- Alternatively, his mother may have been a Schildknecht who married into the Kontze family, and the pastor initially wrote the maternal surname by mistake
- The correction was likely made at the time of the baptism (same ink, same hand), not a later emendation
