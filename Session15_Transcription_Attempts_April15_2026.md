### What was NOT found

- **No entry labeled "Maria Carolina Konze"** with Konze as father's surname anywhere in images 1739–1745.
- **Justus Ernst Konze baptism (14 Nov 1829)** — expected on images 1747–1748, not reached at zoom level this session.

### Interpretation

If our target "Maria Carolina Konze b.1828" genuinely exists in Deisel church records, her baptism might be:
- (a) Entry Z.12 Sophia Carolina — if "Sophia" was a first baptismal name later dropped and "Carolina" retained, with the **mother** (Maria Elisabeth geb. Konzen) being our "Maria Carolina Konze" source of the Konze surname via maternal line; but this makes her a Hildebrand, not a Konze child.
- (b) Entry Z.14 Maria — if the father reading is "Ludwig Konze" rather than "Konrad"; but the child is just "Maria" and died 1835.
- (c) Elsewhere — in a Konze entry we missed at current zoom, or in another 1828 section.

Research implication: The "Maria Carolina Konze" identity hypothesis needs re-examination against the evidence. Session 13 had recorded expansion of the Joh. Hofhoff Konze children (Johannes, Joh. Hofhoff, Maria Magdalena, Anna Catharina) — "Maria Carolina" may not actually be among the confirmed Konze children of Deisel and could be a misattribution from earlier research.
