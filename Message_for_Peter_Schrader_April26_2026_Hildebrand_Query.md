## The Specific Record I Am Looking For

I am searching for the **marriage record of Johann Heinrich Konze × Wilhelmine Hildebrand**, the parents of a child born in Deisel on January 23–24, 1835. The baptism entry in the Deisel Kirchenbuch (Register 218842, Entry 195) records:

- **Child's name:** Justus (male)
- **Father:** Joh. Heinrich Konze, Kaufmann (merchant), Deisel
- **Mother:** Wilhelmine, geborene Hildabrand [sic]

This entry was written in 1835. The father is described as a Kaufmann — higher social standing than a typical Ackermann, which is unusual in Deisel records of this period. The mother's maiden name is spelled "Hildabrand" (with an 'a'), which I read as Hildebrand.

Given the son's birth date (January 1835, in Deisel), the marriage most likely took place between approximately **1828 and 1834** — and possibly as early as 1822 if there were earlier children.
