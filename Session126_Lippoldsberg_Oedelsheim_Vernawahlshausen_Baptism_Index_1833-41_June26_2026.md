# Session 126 — Baptism-Index Checks: Lippoldsberg, Oedelsheim, Vernawahlshausen (1833–1841)
## Date: June 26, 2026

## Objective
Check the **baptism registers/indexes** of three Weser-valley Kreis-Hofgeismar
parishes — **Lippoldsberg, Oedelsheim, Vernawahlshausen** — across **1833–1841**
for: (a) **Charles** (a boy **Carl/Karl born 26 Jan 1835**, surname hypothesised to
anglicise to "Gannsen"); and (b) any **Konze-pool surname** (Kontze/Konze/Konzen,
**Gans/Ganß/Ganson**, Jungkontze). All three had their **confirmation** registers
checked earlier but **not** their baptisms. Klaus Kunze's OSB attests, for these
parishes: **Lippoldsberg** = Kontze, Jungkontze, Gans, Ganß, Ganson; **Oedelsheim**
= Kontze, Kontzen, Konzen, Jungkonz, Gans, Ganß; **Vernawahlshausen** = Gans only.

Driven live in the logged-in Archion browser (subscription content). Method: where
the alphabetical index was legible/AI-transcribed, read the K + G (+ J) sections;
where the index was faded/shuffled, sweep the 1833–41 baptism leaves directly using
the viewer's AI "Erkannter Text" panel, regex-scanning every leaf for Gans/Konze
surnames and Carl/Karl.

## 1. LIPPOLDSBERG — Taufen 1830–1870 (Archion churchRegister 219778) — NEGATIVE
**Clean result via the alphabetical index** (legible and AI-transcribed).
- Calibration: **baptism Seite = image − 1411** (verified: Bild 1510 = Seite 99 =
  Jahr 1846). The 1833–1841 window ≈ **Seite 20–72**.
- Index location: alphabetical name-index at the end of the reel (A…Z ≈ Bild
  1665–1695). **K = Bild 1680–1681; G = Bild 1678; H = Bild 1679.**
- **K section (Bild 1680–1681): NO Kontze / Konze / Jungkontze.** The K surnames are
  all local: Käse, Kahle, Krapf, Koch, Knuth, Knipping, Kettler, Kramer, Kuhlmann,
  Kühne, Kraft, Kluges, Künemund, Kruse. (Carls present on these — e.g. Käse Karl
  Wilhelm, Kahle Karl Wilhelm, Klages Karl Friedrich Wilhelm Seite 55 ≈ 1838 — none
  a Konze form.)
- **G section (Bild 1678): a Gans family IS indexed, but its earliest Seite is 99 =
  1846** — i.e. **every Gans baptism postdates the 1833–41 window.** Gans entries
  (Seite): Friederike Auguste 99 (1846), [Geins/Gans] Heinrich Konrad Friedrich 110,
  Karoline Wilhelmine 147, **Karl Wilhelm 152 (≈1854), Karl 162 (≈1856)**, + George
  Wilhelm, August Eduard, Johanne Karoline, Frederika Louise, Gerhard — all Seite
  99–165, i.e. **1846–1856**. The two **Carl/Karl Gans boys (1854, 1856) are far too
  late to be Charles (b. 1835)**.
- **VERDICT: NEGATIVE for 1833–1841.** No Kontze/Konze anywhere; the Lippoldsberg
  Gans family only begins baptising in **1846**. (Consistent with the earlier note
  that Lippoldsberg confirmations *do* carry Gans — those confirmands were born
  ~1845+ / baptised from 1846.)

## 2. OEDELSHEIM — Taufen 1830–1877 (Archion churchRegister 219946) — PARTIAL / NOT cleanly eliminated
**The index is partly unusable and the register is shuffled**, so this parish could
NOT be cleanly cleared; a dedicated follow-up is flagged.
- **Index problem:** the alphabetical **K(ontze) leaf (Bild 918) is badly faded**
  (iron-gall ink loss + bleed-through); the AI transcription returns **0 K entries**
  ("K / Seite / —"). The G leaf (Bild 915) shows Gronemann, Gassmann (no "Gans"
  seen); the H leaf (916) transcribes fine. So **Kontze cannot be confirmed or
  excluded from the index.**
- **Register is shuffled:** image order does NOT track year (e.g. Bild 705 reads
  "Jahr 1831" sitting *after* Bild 693 ≈ 1836/37), and the OCR of the baptism pages
  is noisy. A clean leaf-by-leaf sweep is therefore laborious.
- **Sampled baptism leaves** (legible, AI-transcribed): Bild 664 (1832/33), 665
  (1833 Jan), 676 (1834), 677–678 (**1834–1835, incl. Charles's exact Jan–Feb 1835
  window**), 693 (1836/37), 705 (a displaced 1831 leaf). RESULT: **NO baptized
  Kontze/Konze/Gans child** on any sampled leaf. Carls present, all on local
  surnames: e.g. **Heinrich Carl Schraeder** (innkeeper's son, b. Oct 1834),
  **Friedrich Carl Wessel** (Hausnr 21, b. ~25 Feb 1835 — rufname Friedrich, not
  Carl), Carl Dettmar (a father), Carl August. **No boy with rufname Carl born 26
  Jan 1835 on a Konze/Gans surname.**
- **⚠️ But the Gans surname IS locally present** — it recurs as **godparents** in the
  1830s (e.g. "Carl August Gans", "Andreas Gans" as Taufpathen on the Bild-705
  leaf), with the baptized children's fathers being Lindner/Schnadhorst/Süg/Koch
  (not Gans). So unlike Sielen/Lippoldsberg, **Oedelsheim has Gans people in the
  parish during Charles's era** and cannot be declared a clean negative on the
  sampling alone.
- **VERDICT: PARTIAL.** No baptized Konze/Gans child found in the sampled window
  (incl. Jan–Feb 1835), but the faded K-index + shuffled register + Gans-as-godparents
  mean a **definitive answer needs a dedicated shuffle-aware full-window leaf sweep
  and/or a Kurrent specialist reading the faded K(ontze) index leaf (Bild 918).**
  FLAGGED FOLLOW-UP. (Aligns with the Oedelsheim research-log hypothesis that the
  OSB Kontze/Gans pool may have largely emigrated/died out before the 1840s — but
  the godparent Gans entries show some Gans presence persisted.)

## 3. VERNAWAHLSHAUSEN — Taufen 1830–1869 (Archion churchRegister 220261) — effectively NEGATIVE
OSB attests **only "Gans"** here (weakest Konze signal of the three).
- Calibration: **Seite = image − 1296** (Bild 1500 = Seite 204 = Jahr 1863; title
  page Bild 1296). The register is **roughly linear / not shuffled** (1327 = 1830/31,
  1349 = 1839/40, 1500 = 1863), though the **page-header year OCR is noisy** (several
  leaves misread as "1837/1838" when context says early-1830s). Small parish,
  ~2–3 leaves/year ⇒ the **1833–1841 window ≈ Bild 1328–1351**.
- Index is **faint and out of alphabetical order** (Bild 1564 = M, Bild 1568 = L),
  so the index route was abandoned in favour of a direct baptism sweep.
- **Swept 8 baptism leaves across the contiguous window block** — Bild 1328, 1330,
  1332, 1334, 1338, 1340, 1345, 1349 (which brackets 1830/31 → 1839/40, i.e. the full
  1833–1841 window): **every leaf KONZE/GANS = NONE.** The only Carls are on local
  surnames / in godparent contexts (e.g. "Carl Friedrich August" ~1838, "des
  Ackermanns Carl", "Carl Ludwig Henne", "Carl Bußmann"). **No Gans/Konze baptized
  child; no Carl with a Gans/Konze surname.**
- **VERDICT: effectively NEGATIVE.** No Gans/Konze baptism in 8 samples spanning the
  window; not exhaustively leaf-by-leaf swept (a handful of intervening leaves
  unread) and the index is faint, so a small residual remains, but the signal is a
  clean negative.

---

## Bottom line
**No Charles** — i.e. no boy with rufname **Carl/Karl born 26 Jan 1835 on a
Konze/Gans surname** — was found in any of the three parishes' 1833–1841 baptisms.
- **Lippoldsberg = clean NEGATIVE** (no Kontze; Gans family baptises only from 1846).
- **Vernawahlshausen = effectively NEGATIVE** (no Gans/Konze in 8 window samples).
- **Oedelsheim = PARTIAL / not cleared** — no baptized Konze/Gans child in the
  sampled window (incl. Charles's exact Jan–Feb 1835 leaves), **but the Gans surname
  is present locally as godparents in the 1830s**, and the K(ontze) index leaf is too
  faded to read, so Oedelsheim stays **open** pending a dedicated full sweep /
  Kurrent read.
