## ⭐ HEADLINE RESULT — GIESELWERDER IS *NOT* CLEAN OF THE KONZE POOL

Gieselwerder Taufen 1830–1881 (219016) is **"mit Register"** — full alphabetical
name-index at the reel end. Reading it overturns the parish's prior baptism-track
"eliminated" status: the index lists a real **Kuntze / Kontze family** AND a large
**Kunze clan** baptising children in Gieselwerder across exactly Charles's era.

**For Charles specifically: still NO direct match** — there is no male Carl/Karl on a
Konze-pool surname born 1833–1841. But the find is a genuine new lead and the parish
must be upgraded from "eliminated" to **active Konze-pool locus (follow-up needed).**

### Gieselwerder calibration (verified live)
Taufen title card = **Bild 1244** ("Gieselwerder * / 1830–1881 / Bem.: mit Register").
**Seite = Bild − 1246** (one written Seite per spread). Verified anchors:
Bild 1263 = S17 = März/April **1834**; 1280 = S34 = Okt/Nov **1835**;
1282 = S36 = Jan–März **1837**; 1373 = S127 = **1854**.
(Year-rate is non-linear — 1834/35 leaves are very dense, likely Erst+Zweitschrift —
so each target Seite's year was read directly, never extrapolated.)

### Index location (reel end), letter→Bild map

F|G = 1511; G|H = 1512; H|I·J = 1513; **I·J tail | K = 1514**.
K-index leaf permalink (minted, ⚠️ canvas was stale at mint — verify; Bild 1514 is the

### Index findings by section
- **C** (Bild 1507R primary = **blank/empty**; 1508L = 5 B-surname overflow entries
  Becker/Bachmann S252–257). **NO Contze / Conze / Conson.** (Gieselwerder's Kunze-OSB
  "Contze/Contzen/Conze" spellings do NOT appear under C in this 1830–1881 baptism index;
  the living family here spells with **K**.)
- **G** (Bild 1511R): **large Gans population** (males at Seiten 25, 62, 68, 82, 86, 95;
  many female Gans too) — but **NO male Carl/Karl Gans** in any year. The only Carls in G
  are Gerland Carl Wilhelm (S76) and Götze Carl August (S107) — non-pool.
- **I·J** (Bild 1513R): only **2 entries** (Friedr. Wilhelm + Adolf Heinrich on an "Ibe/Jbe"
  surname), empty female column → **NO Jungkonze / Jungkontze.**
- **K** (Bild 1514R) — the key section. Grouped by surname:
  - **Kontze** (male): *todtgeboren* (stillborn) **S17**; **Johann Georg Wilhelm S36**.
  - **Kontze** (female): Wilhelmine Friederike **S256** (very late, ~1870s).
  - **Kunze** (large clan, male S67–S252; female S7–S245). The **only Carl in the whole
    pool = "Kunze, Carl Heinr. Albert — S127 = 1854"** (verified out of window by 19 yrs).

### ⭐ The lead: the Heinrich Ernst Kuntze family (image-verified)
- **Stillborn male, März 1834** (Seite 17 / Bild 1263): father **Heinrich Ernst Kuntze**,
  mother **Sophie Dorothea geb. Warnecke**.
- **Johann Georg Wilhelm Kuntze, b./bapt. Februar 1837** (Seite 36 / Bild 1282): father
  **Heinrich Ernst Kuntze**, mother read as **Sophie Elisabeth geb. Reitz** — i.e.
  **possibly a different (2nd) wife** → possible remarriage after the first wife died
  ~1834–35, which would leave a **child-gap around early 1835**.
- The index shows **no Kontze/Kuntze baptism between S17 (1834) and S36 (1837)** — i.e.
  no recorded Gieselwerder Kuntze child in 1835. Charles (b. 26 Jan 1835, surname later
  →Gannsen, no German baptism ever found) would fit that gap *if* a son was born to this
  family ~Jan 1835 and not baptised here (e.g. born just before/after a move, or the
  family emigrated). **Speculative but the strongest Konze-spelled lead in a Weser/Diemel
  parish to date.**

**FOLLOW-UPS (Gieselwerder, now a priority):**

**Gieselwerder VERDICT:** no Charles match in the 1833–1841 baptism index, but the parish
