## 3. Important caveats

1. **Resolution limit.** Non-subscriber Archion view renders the main image at ~470 × 385 px — readable for headers and roughly half of entry text. A subscriber-tier high-resolution pull of the same pages is required to move this finding from "preliminary negative" to "confirmed negative."
2. **Sampled not exhaustive.** 4 of ~16 burial-section pages sampled. The remaining ~12 pages may contain the Kontze entry. A full page-by-page scan is the next action for a subscriber session.
3. **Zeyhl remains unidentified.** Even a confirmed-negative Sielen result does not prove Zeyhl is a filial of Deisel — it only rules out Sielen. Deisel KB 1736–1796 (Register 218821) and especially the Deisel Kirchenbuch-Registerband 1635–1796 alphabetical index (Register 218833) remain the highest-value next targets.
