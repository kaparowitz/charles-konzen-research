She could be a sister, cousin, or aunt of Andreas Kontze.

## Key Analytical Findings from the April 7 Search

### 1. Justus Ernst Konze was in Friedrichsfeld in 1860
The unbaptized male Konze entry (#269) proves Justus Ernst was alive, married, and working as a day laborer in Friedrichsfeld in October 1860. This is our **first adult record** of any of Andreas's sons from his second marriage. It narrows the emigration window: if Justus Ernst = Charles Gannsen, he must have emigrated AFTER October 1860.

### 2. The Kontze family extended to Hilmarshausen
Maria Magdalena née Kontze from Hilmarshausen married a Schleiffer man and appears in the Friedrichsfeld register around 1832. Hilmarshausen is approximately 8 km south of Trendelburg. This shows the Kontze family had branches beyond the Trendelburg-Friedrichsfeld-Deisel triangle.

### 3. The 1844 Carl Wilhelm Konze entry is NOT in the index
The alphabetical index at the end of the baptism section does NOT contain the 1844 Carl Wilhelm Konze entry, despite it clearly existing in the register (confirmed by Peter's photographs). This means **the index is incomplete** — other entries may also be missing from the index.

### 4. Corrected index: 7 entries total, not 5
The April 6 search missed the "Konze, Maria Carol. Philip." entry on page 56 in the Weiblich column of the K index. Combined with the non-indexed Carl Wilhelm Konze entry, the total is now 7 Kontze/Konze baptism entries (plus the Schleiffer/Kontze connection).

### 5. Confirmation timeline: Andreas described as "verschollenen" from 1844 through 1865
All three of Elisa Melusine's children's records describe their grandfather Andreas Konze as "verschollenen" (disappeared):
- 1844: "Tochter des verschollenen Andreas Konze"
- 1850: "Tochter des verschollenen Ackermanns Andreas Konze"
- 1865: "ledige Tochter des weiland verschollenen Andreas Konze"

The 1865 entry adds "weiland" (late/deceased), suggesting the family may have learned of his death by then (he died 1854 in Mainz), but the "verschollenen" descriptor persisted.

### 6. No child born ~1835 to Andreas Kontze — CONFIRMED
The April 6 finding stands: there is NO Kontze/Konze entry between page 13 (August, October 1834) and page 43 (Carl Wilhelm, 1844). **Charles Gannsen was NOT baptized in the Friedrichsfeld register.**
