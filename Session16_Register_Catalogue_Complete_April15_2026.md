### Structural implications

1. **No separate 218822 or 218823 register exists.** The candidate numbers hypothesised in Session 14 are absent from Deisel's catalogue — they may be assigned to neighbouring parishes or be non-digitised.
2. **Register 218821 = Kirchenbuch 1736-1796** covers the full 60-year span, so Andreas Konze b.1.10.1795 MUST be in 218821. Session 15's reported "baptism section terminus ~option 88 / year c.1750" was **misinterpreted** — the option-88 break is an internal sub-register boundary (likely the end of the first sub-book within the bound register), and the 1795 baptisms sit in a later sub-block that needs a fresh targeted scan at options 100–270.
3. **After 1831, Deisel shifted from combined to separated registers**: Taufen (218842), Trauungen (218860), Beerdigungen (218851), Konfirmationen (218836) each start at 1830–1831. This means any 1835 unrecorded Konze child (per the alternative-Charles-Gannsen-origin hypothesis) would be in **218842 Taufen 1830-1861** — a register never previously touched in this research project.
4. **Justus Ernst Konze b.14 Nov 1829** should end up in 218824 (current register) rather than 218842, because 218824 terminates at 1831. However, if the baptism was delayed beyond the 218824 cut-off date, it could appear in 218842 instead.

### Interpretation of the Justus Ernst non-sighting

Three hypotheses remain open:

1. **Delayed baptism in 218842 Taufen 1830-1861** — if Justus Ernst was born 14 Nov 1829 but baptised in early 1830, the entry would sit in the new separated Taufen register rather than in the combined 218824. This is the **highest-priority Session 17 check**.
2. **Entry hidden in the eodem/undated block on image 1746 right page** — the "Carlotta geb. Konze" eodem entry in the late-1829 terminal sequence has its child's name obscured. If that child is male and the naming resembles "Ludwig Justus" or similar, this could be our target. **A dedicated high-zoom pass on that single entry is needed.**
3. **Justus Ernst not from Deisel** — if the 14 Nov 1829 birth date was for a child baptised in Trendelburg or another parish, the 218824 search will never find him. The catalogue search (Move 3) showed Deisel has 16 registers; other Kurhessen parishes (especially Trendelburg) need parallel searches.

### New Konze-mother data point

Session 15 confirmed two 1828 Konze-mother baptisms (Z.12 Sophia Carolina with mother Maria Elisabeth geb. Konzen; Z.14 Maria with mother Wilhelmina geb. Konzen). Session 16 adds a **third Konze-mother baptism in late 1829** with mother "Carlotta geb. Konze". Across 1828–1829 Deisel, the running count is now:

- Maria Elisabeth geb. Konzen (Hildebrand husband) — ~Nov 1828
- Wilhelmina geb. Konzen ([surname ambiguous] husband) — 1828
- **Carlotta geb. Konze ([surname obscured] husband) — late 1829**

This pattern of three Konze-born women each married into different non-Konze Deisel families suggests the Konze surname was moderately common in the village's marriageable generation c.1795–1810. Any of these could be siblings or cousins of Andreas Konze b.1795 or of the Kontze/Konze male line.
