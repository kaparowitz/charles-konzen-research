## Register structure & calibration (established this session)

219097 is a **dedicated confirmation book** (not a combined volume) and — unlike
the shuffled/duplicated Hofgeismar reels (219172/219742/219673/219532/219046) — it
is **cleanly, chronologically ORDERED**. Confirmation section title page = **Bild
1826** ("Grebenstein Konf. 1831–1876"); first class (1831) at Bild 1827.

- **Two written Seiten per image; Seite(left page) = 2 × image − 3656.**
  Verified anchors: img 1845 = S34/35 (Jahr 1834); img 1900 = S144/145 (1848);
  img 1901 = S146 (1848) / S147 (1849); img 1903 = S150/151 (1849); img 1904 =
  S152/153 (1850).
- **Classes are very LARGE** (~50 confirmands/year → ~5 Seiten ≈ 2.5 images per
  class). Each class = boys ("**A. Söhne**", Nro 1…N) first, then girls
  ("**B. Töchter**", Nro restart 1…M). Year is printed on every page header
  ("Seite N — Jahr 18XX (date)"); a "Confirmirten am Pfingsten 18XX" sub-heading
  marks each class start. **Surnames underlined**; confirmation pages are NOT
  AI-transcribed ("txt" panel empty) — read by eye.
- **Viewer quirk:** classic stale-canvas after a page-number jump (blank or
  previous leaf). Fix: after the jump click the thumbnail in the left strip and/or
  a zoom +/− to force a redraw, then read. The zoom slider does NOT reliably
  snap to fit — clicking "+" once over-magnifies and re-centers on the gutter;
  to return to fit click "−" ~4×. Reliable reads came from: jump → redraw →
  "−" to fit → screenshot → `zoom` action on the name/birth columns.
