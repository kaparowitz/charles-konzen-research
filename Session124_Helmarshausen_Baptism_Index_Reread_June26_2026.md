# Session 124 — Helmarshausen baptisms 1833–1841 re-read (Archion reg. 219214)

**Date:** 26 June 2026
**Register:** Archion churchRegister **219214** — Helmarshausen, Taufen 1830–1856 "mit Register"
(Kirchenkreis Hofgeismar; 598 images)
**Prompt:** "check the helmarshausen baptisms 1833–1841."

---

## Why re-open a parish already eliminated

Helmarshausen was eliminated in **April 2026**, but on a thin basis:
an **index-only** K-section read plus a Jan–Feb 1835 spot-check. That read

- predated the **26 Jun 2026** decision to treat **Gans / Gansen / Ganson** as
  in-pool (n-s-n skeleton; "Ganson" is in the surname canon), so G was never
  checked; and
- sat in tension with the **Helmarshausen Ortssippenbuch** (Klaus Kunze), which
  *does* list **Konze** (fam. 67, 96, 302, 406) and **Jungkontz / Jungkonze**
  (297, 507, 468) in the parish.

This is exactly the configuration that overturned the earlier "no Kontze in
Hümme" finding — so a higher-resolution re-read was warranted.

## Method

Drove the Archion viewer (subscriber login). The register is **linear /
chronological**, ~1 written Seite per image, ~13 images/year; baptism title page
= Bild 312, baptisms run ~Bild 313–410. Calibration anchors read live:
**1833 ≈ Bild 337** (Mai/Juni, Seite 25, Nro 111–116); **Jan 1835 ≈ Bild 356**
(1835 opens at Nro 201, Seite 42; late-Jan 1835 on Bild 357/Seite 43).

The book's own **alphabetical name-index** (Bild ~579–585, one letter per page)
is the comprehensive surname check across **all** years at once — more reliable
for a surname search than scanning ~110 individual leaves, and the "any Carl
born 1833–41" angle is already covered by the completed confirmation sweep
(reg. 219208). Read the K, I/J and G sections at high zoom, then verified the
1835 birth window at page level.

*Viewer notes for next time:* stale-canvas after a page-number jump (force a
redraw with a zoom +/−; prev/next arrows keep zoom and redraw cleanly). The
on-screen `zoom`-region tool **drifts** on this viewer — to read tiny index
text, drag the column to center, click the in-viewer "+" 2–3×, then take a
normal screenshot.

## Verdict

**No Konze / Kontze / Jungkonze / Gans / Ganson baptism exists anywhere in the
Helmarshausen Taufen 1830–1856** (which fully contains the 1833–1841 window), and
no male Carl on a pool surname in the 1835 birth window. The April elimination is
**confirmed and strengthened**, the index-only gap is closed, and the
newly-in-pool Gans/Ganson surname is now also negative.

The OSB Konze / Jungkontz families therefore fall **outside** this baptism book —
their events are pre-1830 or in the marriage / burial registers, not in the
1833–1841 births. **Helmarshausen stays ELIMINATED** on the baptism (index +
1835 leaf), confirmation (219208), and OSB tracks alike.
