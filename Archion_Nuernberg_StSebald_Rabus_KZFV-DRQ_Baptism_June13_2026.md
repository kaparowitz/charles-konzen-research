### Diplomatic transcription (Kurrent → type)
> ☉ d. 12. P. Johann Adam Rabus, Bierwirth. M. Margaretha Dorothea.
> I. Johann Wolfgang Martin. S. Johann Wolfgang Martin Jäger,
> Gold- und Silberdraht-Fabri[k]ant.

### Expansion of the register's abbreviations
- **☉ / leading symbol** — denotes the entry (male child).
- **d. 12** — day of baptism: **12 July 1778** (the entry sits under the "1778 Julius" page header).
- **P.** = *Pater* (father): **Johann Adam Rabus**, *Bierwirth* (beer-innkeeper / publican) — *occupation confirmed at max viewer zoom, 13 Jun 2026.*
- **M.** = *Mater* (mother): **Margaretha Dorothea** (FS gives her fuller name as Margaretha Maria Dorothea, née **Rupprecht**; the couple m. 12 Sep 1775 Nuremberg).
- **I.** = *Infans* (the child): **Johann Wolfgang Martin**.
- **S.** = *Susceptor* (godfather / Pate): **Johann Wolfgang Martin Jäger**, *Gold- und Silberdraht-Fabrikant* (gold- and silver-wire manufacturer). The child was named after his godfather, per custom.

### English translation
> On the 12th [of July 1778]. Father: Johann Adam Rabus, beer-innkeeper.
> Mother: Margaretha Dorothea. Infant: Johann Wolfgang Martin.
> Godfather: Johann Wolfgang Martin Jäger, gold- and silver-wire manufacturer.
