### Emerging picture
The Deisel Konze line recurs across these mid-century burials as an
**Ackermann / Tagelöhner farming family** (Johann Heinrich Konze b. 1790 ×
Wilhelmine George; Philipp Konze; Ludwig Konze) that **intermarried with the
Schildknecht and Köster families** (repeated "geb. Konze" wives). A cluster of
**Konze children died in 1874** (Maria Wilhelmine, Wilhelmine Else, + the Nr. 94
daughter). None of this disturbs the Charles search directly, but it firms up
the Deisel Konze household structure.
