# Session 99 — St. Marcus Registers (live FamilySearch read): Bertha's 1894 confirmation found; older children negative; a separate Janson family; 1887 Charles Konze marriage; communion register not digitized

**Date:** 13 June 2026 · live FamilySearch image read (signed-in browser session)
**Source:** St. Marcus Evangelical Church records, 1847–1981 — FamilySearch DGS **007769184** (film 1503035; confirmations item 4, marriages item 5) and DGS **007769183** (film 1503034; baptisms v.1–3, 1847–1906).
**Objective:** (1) determine whether the "Register of Communion 1877–1897" is accessible; (2) read the St. Marcus confirmation register for any confirmation of Charles & Auguste's children; (3) fully transcribe the 1887 "Charles Konze" marriage spotted in passing.

## Headline results

1. **Bertha's confirmation FOUND.** Charles & Auguste's youngest, **Bertha**, was **confirmed at St. Marcus on Palm Sunday, 18 March 1894** (first communion Easter, 25 March 1894), recorded under the family's Anglicized surname **"Bertha Johnson"** — daughters' (Töchter) entry **16**, birth listed **20 December [1879]**. (DGS 007769184, image **258**, the "1894" class.)
2. **The five older children: negative.** None of Maria Josephine (1868), Katherine (1870), Auguste (1872), Louise (1873), or Erwin Carl/Charles Jr. (1875) appears in their expected confirmation years under *any* spelling — Konzen, Konsen, Gansen, Gonsen, Jansen, **or Johnson**. Most likely not confirmed at St. Marcus (consistent with the family's loose-churching pattern).
3. **A separate Janson family — NOT Charles's children.** Three "Jans(o/e)n" confirmands exist at St. Marcus, but their birth dates do not match any Gannsen child, so they are an unrelated family: **Hermann Janson** (b. 28 Sept 1873, conf. 1886), **Heinrich Jansen** (b. 31 Aug 1875, conf. 1890), **Bertha Jansen** (b. 30 June 1879, conf. 1893).
4. **1887 Charles Konze marriage (new, unindexed).** **Charles Konze** (of St. Louis) m. **Laura Brown** (of Centralia, Ill.) on **6 July 1887**; witnesses **Ernest Konze & Lena Mertz**; "engl. getraut." A *different* St. Louis Konze household (Charles Gannsen m. Auguste Kreichelt in 1866 and stayed married until her 1896 death). (DGS 007769184, image **472**, marriage entry No. **2481/49**.)
5. **The "Register of Communion 1877–1897" is not in the FamilySearch digitization.** Item 4 of DGS 007769184 is the confirmation register, running continuously 1849→1981, immediately followed by the marriages title card ("ST MARCUS MARRIAGES 1847–1908"). FamilySearch's image-group contents list only Baptism / Burial / Marriage / Confirmation — no communion. The communion register survives only as the Eden Archives original; it post-dates Charles's ~1859 arrival by ~18 years and lists communicants by date, not birth-origin, so it cannot resolve the chosen-name / origin question.

## Detail — Bertha's 1894 confirmation

| Field | Content |
|---|---|
| **Class** | 1894 — "Im Jahr unsers Herrn 1894, am 18. März, am Palmsonntag … in der Evang. St. Marcus Kirche … confirmirt"; first communion Ostern, 25 März 1894 |
| **List** | Töchter (daughters), entry **16** |
| **Name as written** | **Bertha Johnson** |
| **Birth (as written)** | **20 December** — placed in the 1879 birth block (entry 17, Laura Catharine Mueller, opens 1880) |
| **Image** | DGS 007769184, image **258** |

**Identification.** "Johnson" is the family's Anglicized surname (Bertha Johnson, FS **KGM3-RDK**), and the "**20 Dec**" birth day matches Charles's 1898 pension declaration ("Bertha Gannson, born 20 December 1880") exactly. The surname + birth day identify her.

**⚠ Birth-year wrinkle (1879 vs 1880).** Three sources now give three readings:
- **Confirmation register (this find): 20 December 1879**
- Pension declaration (1898): 20 December 1880
- Baptism register (St. Marcus p. 574, no. 10269): 25 December 1880

The day/month agree across confirmation and pension (20 Dec); the year is 1879 in the confirmation register vs 1880 in baptism/pension. The site canon is **25 December 1880** (per the baptism), with an errata noting an earlier "1879" reading was corrected to 1880 — this confirmation register is an independent attestation of the 1879 alternative and may be where that reading originated. **The canonical 1880 is not overturned**; the confirmation simply records a conflicting year, to be footnoted.
