Changelog

A reverse-chronological log of every significant finding, transcription, identification, and structural change to the project. Latest at the top. Each entry is anchored by date so collaborators can cite a specific version.

The home page carries the latest three or four findings as a feed; this page is the canonical archive. Entries are tagged by confidence: Confirmed means a primary source is attached, Working is the leading current hypothesis, Eliminated is a candidate ruled out, and Structure records site- and methodology-level changes.
