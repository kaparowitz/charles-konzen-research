The search was executed and the register's internal structure was **corrected and re-mapped**, but the specific 1794–1796 baptism leaves — the pages that would contain Andreas Kontze's 1 Oct 1795 baptism — **could not be rendered**: the Archion tiled viewer repeatedly failed in the image band ~1484–1490 (broken-image placeholders, blank frames, stale/duplicate images), and reloads reset the viewer to the title page. The 1795 baptism is therefore still not retrieved from Archion — but it is **not a missing record**: the register's own catalogue confirms Deisel baptisms run to 1796, and Peter Schräder supplied the 1795 baptism record by email in April 2026.
