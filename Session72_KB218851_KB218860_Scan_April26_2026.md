**Significance:** This is the death entry for Marie Köster, daughter of Ludwig Köster × Wilhelmine née Konze. It confirms:
1. Ludwig Köster × Wilhelmine née Konze were at **Gänsewinkel Nr. 29** (different from our candidate's Nr. 4)
2. This is a **third documented Köster × Konze marriage** in the Deisel/Gänsewinkel community
3. The Wilhelmine Kontze (b. 1800) line in the Klaus Andreas branch (first husband Ludwig Köster) is now confirmed by a primary source death record
4. Ludwig Köster must have died between Marie's birth (November 1, 1828) and Wilhelmine's second marriage to J.H. Schildknecht (May 1832), consistent with prior assessment

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Groom | Carl Friedrich Köster |
| Groom's age | ~26 (born ~1828) |
| Groom's father | [name partially legible] Köster, deceased |

**Significance:** Carl Friedrich Köster (born ~1828) with mother née Konze is almost certainly the **son of Ludwig Köster × Wilhelmine née Konze** at Gänsewinkel Nr. 29 — the same parents as Marie Köster (Entry 155 in KB 218851). Carl Friedrich would have been born ~1828, before Marie's birth on November 1, 1828, or shortly thereafter. His father Ludwig Köster is described as deceased by 1854, consistent with the 1828–1832 death window established from other records. This confirms that Ludwig Köster × Wilhelmine née Konze had at least two children: Carl Friedrich (born ~1828) and Marie (born 1.11.1828, died 1835).
