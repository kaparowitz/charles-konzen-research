Session 93 — Person-level census + exact-date pulls (Jungkonze, 220096 marriage index, Contzen) — 7 June 2026

Scope. Jed asked for the Konze-family-by-town page to be rebuilt with one entry per individual (grouped by family under titles), each with birth/death and marriage dates, spouse and children, all sourced with an Archion reference — and for the missing exact dates to be pulled from the registers. Two parallel Archion read sessions (own Chrome tabs) executed the pulls; the page was then rebuilt person-by-person (146 individual rows).

A. Pull 1 — Trendelburg 220108 / 220099 / 220102 (1819–1840s)

Calibration correction: in register 220108 the relationship is Seite = image − 1378 (one full table-Seite per image). The old memory note "image = 1375 + option" mis-mapped several Seiten (e.g. image 1420 is Seite 42, not 28).

C. The Jungkonze family, reconstructed (1755–1837)

Roots in Stammen (Christoph Jungkonze, d. before 1755; Joh. George Jungconze zu Stammen, d. before 1801). Trendelburg households: the Fruchtmesser Joh. Henrich (~1740–1808, recorded 1783 as Corporal bey den Königlichen Husaren) and the Amtsdiener → Schneidermeister line (Amtsdiener Johannes → Johannes the Schneidermeister m. 1792 Carolina Charlotte Enders → children Ludwig the Schneider × Marie Meerkamm, Wilhelmine × Grosberndt, George the artillery NCO at Cassel, Maria Elisabeth the unmarried mother, and probably Johannes the artillerist/Weißbinder, father of Eduard b. 1822). All five of Ludwig's documented children's dates are now exact; three died as small children. The family repeatedly returns to Stammen and Cassel — consistent with the Stammen Jungkontze maiden names of 1835 (Pappenheim and Schäfer wives).

Relevance to Charles: none of the newly-dated Jungkonze/Konze children matches a 26 Jan 1835 birth. The two 1835-born boys in these registers (Carl Wilhelm Ludwig Jungkonze, b. 18 Jun 1835; Ludwig Friedrich Konze, Entry 194 Deisel, b. early Jan 1835 — reading flagged) remain non-matches. The no-candidate status is unchanged.

E. New open questions

  1. ⚠ Johann Friedrich Kontze's father: Deisel Küster Andreas I vs. Trendelburg Rathsverwandter Andreas — the 1795 marriage favors the latter; the Deisel↔Trendelburg link needs re-proof.
  2. 220096 S.385 (img ~1190) "Jungkontze Joh. Georg" burial — film-seam; revisit.
  3. Ludwig Jungkonze (Schneider) — his own baptism (~1795–1800, expect 220096) and death not yet read; ditto Maria Elisabeth Jungkonze's birth.
  4. Whether the 1834-godfather Johannes Jungkonze (Weißbindergesell zu Stammen) = the artillerist Johannes (d. before Apr 1836), Eduard's father.
