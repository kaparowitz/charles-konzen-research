Session 96 — Christoph Kontze (b. 13 Feb 1814, Hümme): US-side trail — 7 June 2026

Scope. Jed asked to (1) add the Konde/Konten spellings to the by-town page's variant list and (2) find Christoph Kontze — the Hümme emigrant named in Session 95 — and where he went. Searched: FamilySearch record search + full-text + Family Tree (signed-in session), web search. The HESAUS/LAGIS emigrant database was already searched in full on 25 May 2026 (no Christoph; the only Konze hit is Ludwig Konze, Deisel → Frankfurt 1864).

Target profile. Christoph Kontze, b. 13 Feb 1814 Hümme, son of the late Ackermann Friedrich Kontze × Marie Sophie geb. Busch; confirmed Pfingsten 25 May 1828; orphaned; brothers Johannes (b. 1810, → Stammen, d. 1871) and Friedrich (b. 1817, d. 1897, stayed). The only adult Konze male absent from Hümme's complete marriage (1830–91) and burial (1830–74) indexes.

A. Two unverified US candidates found

Candidate 1 — "Christopher Kontze," Hardin County, Ohio (1840–1847)

  • Ohio Tax Records 1800–1887: "Christopher Kontze," tax assessments 1840–41 and 1844, Hardin, Ohio (FS arks 1:1:7DWC-36ZM and 1:1:789P-R6N2). The spelling Kontze — rare in the US — is exact.
  • Continuation: "Christopher Kuntz," 1847 tax, Pleasant Township, Hardin Co. (same collection) — same man, spelling drifting to the common Kuntz.
  • Gone by 1850: the 1850 census of Hardin Co. has NO Christopher under Kuntz/Koontz/Kontze/Counts (only US-born Koontz/Kuhns families). He left the county (or died) 1847–50.
  • Fit: timing matches Christoph's 1840s disappearance (he'd be 26 in 1840). Identity unproven — no record gives age or birthplace.

Candidate 2 — "Christian Kontze," Atlanta, Georgia (1850)

  • 1850 census, Atlanta, DeKalb Co., GA (household 338): Christian Kontze, 34, b. ~1816 Germany; wife Mary, 36, Germany; daughter Amelia, 5, b. New York (ark 1:1:MZY2-5ZF; duplicate index as "Marthasville"). → family arrived in the US by ~1844-45 (child born NY).
  • Fit: "Christian" for Christoph is a plausible enumerator slip; age off by two years (census-normal). Unproven.
  • No trace after 1850 under Kontze/Konze/Kuntze in Georgia or via the daughter (searches for Amelia b. ~1845 NY with father Christian in 1860 = negative).
  • Eliminated red herring: the Ontario "Amelia Kontze × Duncan McFarlane" trail is a DIFFERENT Amelia (b. 1863 Ontario, dau. of Johann Carl David Kuntze 1819–1911 of Elgin Co. — FS 9CJ8-PQG). The 1860 "C F Kuntze" of Warren Co., Missouri = Charles F. Kuntze, wife Laura — also not him.

Verdict

Christoph emigrated leaving almost no name-faithful US paper trail. The two candidates are logged on the by-town page as unverified; neither contradicts the other (Hardin man could even have moved on), but neither has a record naming age, birthplace, or kin. No FS full-text hit, no FS-tree person, no HESAUS record exists for him.

B. Bonus discovery — "GeneDana," an independent FS researcher on the same registers

  • The FS Family Tree contains a substantial Hümme/Trendelburg/Deisel Kontze tree (e.g. Johann Christoph Kontze 1763–1828 Hümme G245-6VQ; Trendelburg Kontzes back to Andreas 1607–1681 GQQN-DKX; Christoph's parents as a skeleton: Johann Friederich Kontze GRC2-K99 × Maria Sophia Busch G2DM-YFC, no children attached, 0 sources).
  • Person GPK7-JYP "Friedrich Kontze" (= the project's Friedrich b. 19 Sep 1816, Leineweber, son of Johannes Kontze "gebürtig von Stammen" × Lichtefeld) carries collaboration notes by user "GeneDana" (Feb 2024) with verbatim Archion transcriptions of the 1856 Hümme marriage (KB Trau 1830–91, S.78 #216) and the 1837 confirmation of brother Heinrich — independently confirming the project's Session-95 readings, including "Johannes Kontze Leinweber geburtig von Stammen."
  • ⚠ Conflict flagged on the page: GeneDana's entry says Friedrich "d. after 1870, New York City" — unsourced — vs. the project's register read d. 5 Aug 1873, Hümme (219538 S.255). Register reading preferred; the NYC claim needs a source (possibly a misattached 1870 census hit).
  • Action item: GeneDana is a potential collaborator (like Peter Schräder) — contactable via FS messaging from GPK7-JYP's collaborate tab.

C. Page edits (germany/konze-family-by-town.html)

  1. Intro variant list now includes Konde / Konten (pre-1770 scribal forms, preserved as written).
  2. Christoph's row: US-side sweep summary added (two unverified candidates + negatives + still-open leads).
  3. Friedrich-son-of-Johannes row: GeneDana/GPK7-JYP death-place conflict flagged.

D. Remaining leads (queued)

  1. HStAM Marburg query (drafted 25 May 2026, ready to send) — ask for Christoph Kontze b. 1814 alongside the Carl/Charles ~1859 request (Best. 180 Hofgeismar emigration/release files are not in HESAUS).
  2. NY passenger arrivals ~1838–45 for Kontze/Kuntze (Christ* given names) — FS collection-level search errored this session; retry, and Ancestry's Hamburg/Bremen indexes.
  3. Hardin Co., Ohio tax originals (the indexed entries have images: acreage/township could profile the man); Hardin Co. deed grantor index 1847–50 for his departure.
  4. Atlanta thread: 1853+ Fulton Co. records, GA deaths, city directories for Kontze/Kuntze.
  5. Ask GeneDana for their source for "after 1870 New York City" (and share the 1873 burial).

E. Addendum (same session) — FS tree PIDs matched to the by-town page

Jed asked for GeneDana's FamilySearch IDs to be matched to the page's entries. Tree sweeps (surname Kontze/Konze × Trendelburg, Deisel, Hümme, Stammen; surname Jungkonze/Jungkontze; ~150 persons recovered) → 65 rows of germany/konze-family-by-town.html now carry †-marked PID chips, with ~30 flagged "FS tree†" notes where the tree adds or contradicts facts. Legend updated; tree data treated as unsourced leads, register readings preferred.

Highest-value tree-only facts (to verify in registers): 1. Heinrich Christoph Jungkonze (b. 25 May 1827) — m. 10 Dec 1866 Aplerbeck (Westphalia) × Catharina Elisabeth Wiegand; 7 children 1867–80; descendants at Dortmund/Recklinghausen to 1985 (KZ56-RBS). Closes a "not yet traced." 2. Heinrich Wilhelm Kontze (1832–58) — m. 15 Jun 1856 STAMMEN × Ulrike Melusine Pappenheim (1835–97) (PZFD-1X4); page said "no marriage recorded." Verify in 220078. 3. Hümme Jungkontze line discovered (not in the project's reconstruction): Christoph → Georg (bap. Hümme 1701) → Johann Georg (1738–92 × Büngener) → Johann Christoph (1768–1824 × Korff) → incl. Wilhelmine m. 2 Jan 1825 Konrad Papenheim (d. 1881) = the Stammen 1835 "Wilhelmine Pappenheim geb. Jungkontze" (KZPG-7DL); also Johannes 1808–65 → Johannes 1838–1907 (m. Hümme 1866). 4. Amalie Konze (1818–45): tree husband = Johannes Sasse (1815–46), m. 9 Oct 1837 (GKDW-K6G) — resolves the Paffe/Sasse conflict toward Sasse; the "Paffe" groom-surname reading is likely wrong. (Also collapses the page's separate "Amalia Sasse née Konze b. 1813" row candidate — review.) 5. Klaus Andreas's 1833-marrying son: tree has Johann Christoph Konze 1802–45 m. 19 Jul 1833 Maria DRÖNNER (not Brümmer), son Ludwig b. 1836 → Frankfurt (9F7F-7M3/9F7F-Q16, m. 11 Nov 1864 Frankfurt × Schleifer, d. 1876) — contradicts the Bürgermeister-line attribution of Ludwig-1836. Register re-read queued. 6. Catharina Elisabetha Kontze (1771–1839, dau. of Johann Henrich 1747–92) m. 19 Feb 1789 Andreas Schildknecht (1762–1807), ×2 Helwig Weiffenbach (G9T9-GFM) — a REAL Konze×Schildknecht couple, challenging the "alias flipped" hypothesis; she = the page's Weisenbach widow. 7. Smaller: Johannes Kontze jr. (1794) m. 29 Apr 1832 Henze, d. 1840; Maria Caroline Konze (1826) m. 1846 Grossberndt, d. 1905; Anna Margretha × Lotz d. 12 Jan 1781 (source of the old "Jan 1781" index confusion); Joan Georg Jungkonze's son Johann Heinrich b. 1766 → Paderborn m. 1811; Carl Ludwig Konze has a tree-only 5th child Johann Heinrich Konze 1837–1907 (99Z3-DNS) not yet on the page.

Conflicts flagged on rows (register reading kept): Küster's wife Brandt vs. tree Temme(+Guthmann), d. 2 vs 21 Mar 1791 (G9DK-DLV); Wilhelmine-b.1800 d. ~1847 vs tree 21 Jul 1843 (GHKF-CVC); Heinrich V. wife Ohldhunst vs tree Schildknecht (GC4P-4Y1); Christine Wilhelmine Philippine b. 11 vs 8 Aug 1834 (99Z3-DFR); Doenges vs tree "Tonjes" (GH23-989); Philipp 1r b. 20 Nov vs tree 20 Aug 1825 (GH8K-6HH); the two Heinrich×Thiele rows = one tree household (G41K-C3S).

Not in the tree (checked): Christoph Kontze b. 1814 (the emigrant), Arnold Philipp Kontze and his elder line, the Rathsverwandter Andreas (d. 1800), Konrad Konze 1780–1841, the swineherd Andreas × Garland couple. The tree's gen-0 also extends earlier than the project's (Georg Kontze ~1575 Gieselwerder → Andreas 1607–1681 Trendelburg, GQQN-DKX) — unsourced, worth a 220093 verification pass.

G. The last four opens — register-resolved (same night, continued)

  1. Küster Andreas I's marriages (218821, register-verified): ×1 Elisabetha Temme (Christian Temme's daughter), m. 23 Feb 1739 "in aedibus Sponsi" — she d. 6 Mar 1772 ("54 J 9 M 8 T", b. ~Jun 1717); ×2 Maria Guthmann (the Schulmeister's daughter), m. 12 Feb 1773. Konrad's baptism (16 Oct 1780, img 1440) names "Ux. Anna Maria, geb. Gutmaennin" + later cross-ref to his 1841 burial; his 1794 confirmation calls the Küster deceased. The Küster d. 21 Mar 1791, 9 p.m., bur. 23 Mar, Nr. 1 ("72 Jahr 6 Monath") — corrects "2 Mar". His father (per the 1739 marriage): the late Christoph Cüntz — supporting the FS tree's Christoph-father (and possibly the Trendelburg Christoph Kontze 1680–1736, G9DK-6W1, who married at DEISEL 1713 — would re-establish the Deisel↔Trendelburg link one generation earlier; to verify). "Brandt" fully withdrawn. FS tree right on all points.
  2. Philipp Konze 1r's baptism FOUND (218824, img 1731): "§.[1]4. Philipp, des [Vorstehers?] … Konze … et uxoris Maria Elisabeth geb. Schrage, geb. d. 20. mens. praet." under the Dezbr. day-marker, margin "†… Februar 1872" — i.e. bap. ~4/5 Dec 1825, b. 20 Nov 1825 ✓. The earlier session's reading was right; the 1849 marriage age (24y 2m 8d) is wrong by three months. RESOLVED in favor of the baptism. (Note: an initial scan of the wrong year-pages (1826) found nothing — the 218824 viewer serves stale canvases aggressively; every read was re-verified after forced redraws.)
  3. Ludwig-1836's parents READ IN HIS OWN BAPTISM (218842 E268, img 52, Deisel Nr. 40): "Männl: Ludwig Konze… b. October der fünfundzwanzigste (5 p.m.), bap. Oct 26 — Joh. Christoph Konze, Ackermann, und dessen Ehefrau Maria geb. Drönner; godfather Ludwig Köster, 'Schwager des Vaters'." The Köster godfather (husband of Klaus Andreas's daughter Wilhelmine) clinches it: Ludwig-1836 → Frankfurt is the son of the YOUNGER Joh. Christoph (1802–45) × Maria Drönner, NOT the Bürgermeister. The Bürgermeister's "×3 Maria Drönner" attribution is an artifact of conflating the two Joh. Christophs. (FS tree right.)
  4. Heinrich × Thiele rows merged on the page (one household, Steinweg 135/136¼; children d. 1868, 1876, 1878; + tree daughter Elise Philippina Catharina 1877–1963).

Bonus find (218842 E279): Amalie Konze, b. ~1 Dec 1836 (bap. 11 Dec), daughter of Ludwig Konze [1r] × Amalie Meimbresse, Zeigelstraße 19 — cross-check against the page's "Amalie b. ~Apr 1836, d. 24 Feb 1840" (age-derived); the Dec-1836 baptism is likely the same girl (death-age slightly off) — flag left for the next Deisel pass.

Final scorecard for the whole conflict program (14 questions): FS tree right 8 · register/project right 3 · both wrong 1 (Brandt) · marriage-age error 1 (Philipp) · structural merges 2. Every flag raised in §E/§F is now closed except: Christoph Cüntz = Trendelburg G9DK-6W1? (new, queued); the Amalie-1836/1840 cross-check; and the unchanged big-ticket items (Johann Friedrich's father conflict; Charles himself).

H. Postscript — the last flag resolved: Ohldhunst → SCHILDKNECHT

218860 E465 (img 1430), Deisel, 9 May 1880 (Sonntag Exaudi; civil Nr. 8/1880; Aufgebote Judica + Palmarum, 14/21 Mar): "Heinrich Konze, Ackermann von Deisel, ehel. Sohn des Ackermanns Ludwig Konze 2t und dessen Ehefrau Wilhelmine geborenen Schildknecht, 32 Jahre alt" × "Philippine Charlotte Amalie Schildknecht von Deisel, ehel. Tochter des daselbst verstorbenen Ackermanns [Philipp?] Schildknecht und dessen Ehefrau Amalie gebornen Hillebrand, 19 Jahre alt." Both ages fit the FS tree exactly (Heinrich 1847–1936 GC4P-4Y1; she 1861–89). "Ohldhunst" was a misread — likely a garble of Schildknecht in Maria Martha's 1881 burial (218854, unread part; cross-check queued, low priority). Page row, chart node (regenerated), and errata updated. Note the onomastic tangle: Heinrich's MOTHER was also geb. Schildknecht, and his parents-in-law's marriage echoes the Schildknecht×Hillebrand pairing of the Bürgermeister's era — Deisel's Konze/Köster/Schildknecht/Hillebrand four-family knot continues to the register's last decades.

Final conflict scorecard (15 questions): FS tree right 9 · project right 3 · both wrong 1 · marriage-age error 1 · structural merge 1.
