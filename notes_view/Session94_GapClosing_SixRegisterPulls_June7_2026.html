Session 94 — Gap-closing: six parallel register pulls + page integration — 7 June 2026

Scope. Jed: "I see many with no birth and death — close the gaps; if no marriage or children is confirmed — explicitly state it" and "tie up any loose ends." Six Archion pulls ran in two waves (220096 burials; 220096 baptisms 1792–1814; Deisel 218860/218824 marriages; Deisel 218824 burial+baptism indexes; Deisel 218851/218842; Trendelburg 220093 earliest generation). germany/konze-family-by-town.html now carries 200 individual person entries, each with explicit "no marriage found / no children recorded / died in infancy — never married" statements where applicable.

A. Generation zero found (Trendelburg, 220093)

  • Arnold Philipp Kontze was baptized 4 March 1708 (220093 S.1 — the first page of the book), son of Herr Andreas Kontze, Kirchen-Senior (b. ~Dec 1672, d. 21/22 Apr 1761, age 88y4m) × Anna Catharina geb. Klieben (d. 2 Aug 1758). Godfather: apothecary Arnold Philipp Uffelmann of Hofgeismar — explaining Arnold Philipp's Uffelmann wife/wives (×1 prob. Anna Elisabeth, d. 1754; ×2 Anna Dorothea, d. 22 May 1785).
  • Brothers: Mathias (bap. 4 Mar 1714); Johann Melcher (bap. 9 Oct 1715, son of "Andreas Contzes jun." — implying an Andreas Sr./jun. pair).
  • Nies (Dionysius) Kontze Sr.: unmarried "juvenis" 1708/09, godfather 1713, son Joh. Daniel bap. 3 Aug 1714; d. before 21 Feb 1759 (burial in unscanned pre-1747 pages). Wife: Catharina Elisabeth geb. Uffelmann (b. ~Jan 1692, d. 21 Feb 1759). Nies jr. ("ledige Geselle Dionysius Konde") b. ~Jan 1738, d. 28 May 1763.
  • Joan Henric Konde = Kirchen-Senior u. Beckermeister, b. ~early May 1705, d. 20 Feb 1762, bur. 23 Feb 1762; married twice (×1: 2 daughters + 1 son; ×2: widow + 2 daughters).
  • Jost Conze — daughter bap. 11 Jan 1709 (a fourth gen-0 householder).
  • "Elder Joh. Henrich Kontze, Rathsverwandter d. bef. 1774": NO burial 1770–75 in either register; the 1769 Rathsverwandter was Andreas jun., 1779 Arnold Philipp — premise flagged (may = the baker d. 1762, posthumously mis-titled).
  • Key cross-link re-read (S.57, 1787): Andreas b.1787's godfather is "ANDREAS KONTZE VON STAMMEN, des Vaters Halbbruder" — ⚠ if the Rathsverwandter Andreas (d. 1800) is the Stammen godfather AND son of Nies, while Joh. Henrich the younger is son of Arnold Philipp, "Halbbruder" cannot be literal paternal half-brothers. Open question; possibly shared mother or loose usage.

B. 220096 burials — every indexed Kontze/Jungkonze read (17 adults + 11 children dated)

Highlights (full table on the by-town page): Arnold Philipp d. 3 Apr 1780 (71); Joh. Henrich the younger d. 29 Apr 1804 (b. ~late Jun 1740 — resolves "d. 1797–1805"); his widow Osthermann d. 23 Apr 1818; Johannes Kontze alive past 1818 (son Johann Henrich, b. 3 Aug 1786, d. 24 May 1818); wife Thielin d. 20 Dec 1804; Rathsverwandter Andreas Contze d. 7 Nov 1800 (b. ~Feb 1723; missing from the register's own index) — wife Anna Christina geb. Contzin d. 7 Mar 1807 → he = "Andreas Contze II/the Elder of Stammen" = prob. the 1760 groom (son of Nies). Elder Joh. Henrich "Konke" — wife is geb. Uffelmann (not "Wilhelm"), he was a Branntweinbrenner, and he died BEFORE Jan 1787 ("weiland" in her burial). Jungkonze: Amtsdiener Joan Georg d. 21 Feb 1797 (b. ~1733 — the S.385 film-seam entry, resolved; = "the late Joh. George Jungconze zu Stammen" of 1801); his widow geb. Kohlstatt d. 10 Feb 1806 (resolving the Kohlstatt attribution); Fruchtmesser Joh. Henrich d. 20 Aug 1808 (~66–68). Women: Anna Margretha Wiechard geb. Kontze (b. ~Dec 1702 — oldest dated Kontze, d. 22 Dec 1777); Maria Christina Kersting geb. Konze (b. ~May 1726, d. 14 Jan 1806 — the 1748 penance bride); Anna Catharina Jordan geb. Contzin (1743–1797, Bürgermeister Jordan's widow — a Kontze–Jordan tie predating the 1795 marriage); Anna Margaretha Albrecht geb. Contzin (b. ~Aug 1749, d. 2 Nov 1800).

C. 220096 baptisms 1792–1814 — complete sweep (every Kontze/Jungkonze-father entry)

  • Johannes Jungkonze × Carolina Charlotte Enders — all six children: Carl Ludwig ("Ludwig" the tailor) b. 24 Jan 1793 (margin † ~Apr 1862); Johann George ("George" the artillery NCO) b. 16 Nov 1794; Joh. Henrich b. 20 Apr 1798 († 1799); twins Wilhelmina + Christina Charlotta b. 21 Mar 1801 (Christina † 7 May 1801; Wilhelmina = the Grosberndt wife, † ~1 Oct 1863); Maria Elisabeth (the unmarried mother) b. 21 Feb 1804; Maria Caroline b. 3 Feb 1807 (m. Wichard; margin: † 1855 zu GOTTSBÜREN — a Jungkonze death in Gottsbüren, added to that page section). NO son Johannes → the artillerist Johannes (Eduard-b.1822's father) is NOT from this couple; parentage open.
  • Andreas Kontze × Wilhelmina Grosbernd — corrected children: Joh. Christoph b. 13 Jun 1808 (margin † 23 Aug 1880), Joh. Friedrich b. 27 Sep 1810, Johannes b. 28 Nov 1814 (margin † 23 Aug 1894). "Anna Maria ~1810" and "Joh. Henrich ~1812" do not exist (complete 1807–14 read) — disentanglement doc corrected.
  • Catharina Elisab. Jungconze: baptism NOT in 1773–Feb 1788 → b. likely 1788–90; ×2 (by 1809) Joh. Hermann Heidt, Fruchtmesser; children Zollmann 1805, Heidt 1809/1811/1814.
  • Grenadier Joh. Christoph Contze ("entlassener Soldat") × Anna Catharina geb. Pfeil von Homberg: 6 children 1799–1811; Maria Elisabeth bap. 14 Apr 1799, † 12 Jun 1834 — the 1825 unmarried mother. Probably = the Deisel Grenadier (House 24), relocated.
  • Stammtafel-line exacts: Andreas b. 1 Oct 1795 bap. 7 Oct (register-confirmed); Anna Christina b. 11 Dec 1800; Carl Ludwig b. 10 May 1804 (margin † 22/1 62 ✓).
  • Earlier sweep S.10–59 (1773–88): Joh. Henrich Jungkontze the Fruchtmesser's wife identified — Martha Elisabetha geb. Köhler; his sons Johann Conrad b. 17 Jul 1774, Christoph Henrich † 10 Jul 1770 (smallpox); the smallpox girl Maria Elisabeth b. exactly 12 Feb 1782. JH-the-younger's children's exact birth dates incl. NEW daughter Anna Catharina b. 13 Feb 1782. Johannes Kontze's children Wilhelmina b. 12 Jan 1782, Anna Catharina b. 25 Nov 1783, Johann Henrich b. 3 Aug 1786. Lotz × Anna Margretha geb. Konzin HAD children (Friedrich 1776, Carl Wilhelm 1778, Anna Catharina 1781). Albrecht × Kontzin children 1776/1779/1783.

D. Deisel — 218824 indexed burials all read; 218851/218842 network dated; marriages found

  • Klaus Andreas Kontze: b. ~29 Jun 1768, d. 24 May 1828 (age 59y10m25d) — the census's separate "young Andreas d. 24 Feb 1828 aged 29" was a misread of THIS entry (only one adult Andreas burial 1796–1831); he was Ackermann u. Kirchenältester, occupation once "Müller"; wife Catharina Elisabeth geb. Thiele (d. ~1832, indexed 218851 S.12). Children corrected: Wilhelmine b. 20 FEB 1800 (not 20 May; the "Catharine bap. 1799" does not exist); Johann Christoph (~1802, m. 19 Jul 1833 Maria Brümmer); Maria Elisabeth b. 16 Mar 1805; Johannes b. 26 Oct 1813 (not 1810), Schäferknecht, d. 15 Aug 1835.
  • TWO Wilhelmine-geb.-Konze women were conflated: Klaus Andreas's daughter (b. 20 Feb 1800) was still the WIFE of Ludwig Köster II at her death, ~21 Jul 1843 (not 1847); the woman who married Joh. Heinrich Schildknecht (the "1832" marriage — not in Deisel's register 1830–34) is a different Wilhelmine geb. Konze (mother in 218842 E225, 1835).
  • More Deisel adults dated (218824 burials): Kirchenältester Johann Christoph Konze sen. (b. 11 Feb 1740, d. 3 Dec 1817) × Catharina Elisabeth Köster (b. 24 Sep 1768, d. 12 Nov 1821) — the 1805 "second Maria Elisabeth's" parents; Johann Christoph jun. (b. 22 May 1774, d. 18 Nov 1814); Christian Konze (b. ~7 Sep 1756, d. 20 Jan 1829) × Anna Dorothea Brönner (d. 1821); an earlier Joh. Christoph d. before Jan 1796 (son Johann Conrad 1777–96); Carl Wilhelm (1776–96, son of a deceased Andreas); the 1792 widow = Anna Catharina geb. KÖSTER, b. ~Aug 1753, d. May 1811; spinster Anna Catharina (1760–1826); Maria Wilhelmine Kuhlmann geb. Kontzin (1790–1811, childbed); Louise Amalia (1828–29, the Bürgermeister's daughter).
  • Marriages (218860): Amalie Konze × Johannes Paffe 9 Oct 1837 (not 1838; she b. ~1818, dau. of Joh. Christoph × Schrage); Charlotte Konze × Adam Heggemann 26 Aug 1838 (b. ~1814, dau. of Christoph × Hillebrand); Philipp #1 (b. ~20 Aug 1825) × Josephine Florentine Schildknecht 28 Oct 1849; Philipp #2 (b. 7 Oct 1824) × Wilhelmine Moritz 14 Jun 1850 — his parents: Johann Heinrich Kontze (b. 9 Jul 1790, d. 30 Jan 1865, 218851 E1198) × Anna Catharina Köster (a SECOND J.H.×A.C.Köster couple, two generations after the 1792 one).
  • Network dated (218851/218842): Konrad Konze d. 22 DEC 1841 (not Nov; entry register-confirms "Andreas Sohn, geb. von Marie Elisabeth Brandt"); his wife d. 3 days later (25 Dec 1841). Ludwig Konze 1r (b. 4 Nov 1808, d. 4 Feb 1875) was Kirchenältester × Amalie geb. Meimbresse. Heinrich Wilhelm Konze b. ~13 Feb 1844 (parents Christoph II × Wilhelmine Schrage), m. 12 Dec 1875 Wilhelmine Heidemeier geb. Niemeyer, d. 6 May 1877 (margin). Ludwig b. 25 Oct 1836 bap. 26 Oct. New households: Ludwig Theodor × Schäfer (child 1871–74); Philipp [Theodor?] × Schildknecht (child 1873–74); Heinrich × Temme (1874); Heinrich × Thiele (1876); Ludwig 3r × Kuhlmann (daughters 1855, 1858); Joh. Christoph × Marie Temme (stillborn 1841); Catharine Elise Steitz geb. Konze d. 13 Sep 1874. "Marie Amalie Auguste b. 1857": NOT in the baptism index — only viable match Marie Elise Amalie b. 30 Apr 1858 (hedged on page).
  • Structural: 218851 ends 22 Apr 1876 (1877 deaths are in the next, un-digitized volume); both Deisel registers hide index-continuation pages on unused letter pages (K→C→X/Z) — unlocks 1846–76 burials and 1843–61 baptisms; burial index gap S.92–117 (~1846–50) unindexed; 218824 has ~29 indexed Konze baptisms (list captured, mostly unread).

E. Corrections — see errata er-2026-06-07-deisel-corrections + er-2026-06-07-trendelburg-gen0

F. Remaining loose ends (queued)

  1. 218824's 29 indexed Konze baptisms — ~24 unread (would complete the Deisel birth count 1796–1831).
  2. 218851 unread indexed entries (Cath. Elis. geb. Thiele S.12; the second "Köster, Wilh. geb. Konze" S.159; Kloppmann S.175; Kuhlmann S.68; geb. Jorden S.70 re-check; males S.43–245) + unindexed window S.92–117.
  3. Catharina Elisab. Jungconze baptism (220096 S.60–130) and index pointers "Kontze Maria 69, Elisabeth 107; Jungkontze Joh. Georg 52".
  4. 220093 pre-1747 burials S.407–445 (Nies Sr.'s burial; "Andreas 18" baptism on damaged S.18/19).
  5. The "Halbbruder" puzzle (A above); the Bürgermeister Joh. Christoph's own death entry (after 1836, likely 218851 S.81 "Joh. Christoph 440" — unread).
