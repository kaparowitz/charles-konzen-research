Session 95 — Loose ends closed: four register pulls; Andreas×Garland SOLVED; Hümme emigrant NAMED — 7 June 2026

Scope. Jed: "complete the remaining loose ends" + "make a note for each person why they are not Charles/or Charles's parent." Four parallel Archion pulls (Deisel 218824 baptisms; Deisel 218851 burials incl. unindexed 1846–50; Trendelburg 220093/220096 stragglers; Hümme 219526/219538/219547). The by-town page now carries 220 person entries, each with a "Why not Charles?" verdict column.

A. ★ BREAKTHROUGH — the unplaced Andreas Kontze × Marie Elisabeth Garland couple is SOLVED

The 1837 Hümme burial of Johannes Kontze, Leineweber (219538 S.47, img 1027) reads: born Stammen, 26 Jul 1788, son of "des verstorbenen Schweinehirten Andreas Kontze und der verstorbenen Marie Elisabeth geb. Gehrland" — the SAME couple named in Christian Ludwig's 1833 Stammen marriage. So: - The family's base was STAMMEN (Andreas was the village swineherd there, 1788); they passed through Gottsbüren (~1806, Christian Ludwig's stated birthplace — his mother's home village). - Christian Ludwig has an elder brother: Johannes (1788–1837), settled Hümme, × Catharine Elisabeth Lichtefeld; descendants: Friedrich (1816–1873, Leineweber, three marriages), Heinrich (1823–42), Margarethe Elisabeth (m. Hoff 1845) — the branch continues in Hümme. - Still to find: Andreas the swineherd's own burial (not in Gottsbüren 1805–33 nor the Stammen 1830+ K-index → likely pre-1830 Stammen KB 220063).

B. ★ The Hümme "1840s emigrant" is NAMED: Christoph Kontze, b. 13 Feb 1814

Confirmation Pfingsten 25 May 1828 (219526 img 1156): "Christoph, des † Ackermanns Friedrich Kontze et ux. † Marie Sophie geb. Busch Sohn, geb. den 13ten Febr. 1814." Orphaned with brothers Johannes (b. 4 Jan 1810; → Stammen, d. 1871) and Friedrich (b. 9 Jan 1817; d. 1897). Christoph alone appears in NO Hümme marriage 1830–91 and NO burial 1830–74 (complete indexes) — the only vanishing adult male; aged 26–35 in the 1840s. The old claim "the LAST Konze male emigrated" was wrong as worded — six adult Kontze men stayed (incl. Wilhelm, golden wedding 1901 + diamond wedding 1911 margin!). Next steps: US records for Christoph Kontze/Konze b. 13 Feb 1814; Kurhessen Auswanderungsakten Kreis Hofgeismar. Hümme line fully reconstructed on the page (24 burials + 19 marriages transcribed; Küster Johann Conrad line; Johann Heinrich 1793–1840 line; Johannes/Uffelmann line; Georg Friedrich line; Laubert + Altmann daughters).

C. ⚠ ERRATA-OF-THE-ERRATUM — Deisel had TWO adult Andreas Konzes (yesterday's merge was wrong)

  • Andreas × Catharina Elisabeth Loßin/Lasse (Steinweg 136): b. ~29 Jun 1768, d. 24 May 1828 (= burial-index "Andreas S.82"); widow b. 20 Feb 1770, d. 24 May 1838. Sons: Johann Christoph "IV" (b. 23 Nov 1796, d. 25 Aug 1873, × Marie Temme), Ludwig (b. 4 Nov 1808 = Ludwig 1r, d. 4 Feb 1875, × Amalie Meimbresse).
  • Klaus Andreas alias Schildknecht × Catharina Elisabeth Thiele (Spiegelstraße 29), Ackermann u. Kirchenältester: b. 11 Oct 1774 — "son of Johann Heinrich Konze" (gen-anchor! plausibly the 1792 Ackermann G8HG-P2S × A.C. Köster) — d. 20 Feb 1847 (218851 S.94 E523); wife b. 6 Dec 1778, d. 14 May 1832 (S.12). The 24-May-1828 death belongs to the OTHER Andreas. Session-94's "collapse" merge retracted; the original "d. 24 Feb 1828 aged 29" reading remains wrong either way.

D. Deisel — 24 indexed baptisms read; burial roster completed (218851 incl. unindexed 1846–50)

  • Bürgermeister Christoph Konze: b. 6 Aug 1784 (son of "Justus(?)/Christian(?) Konze" — flagged), d. 21 Apr 1846 (S.88 E484, Haus 125); widow Maria Elisabeth SCHRAGE (b. 28 Jul 1785, d. 9 Feb 1862 — "Schwenge" index reading resolved). His Schrage children all dated (Wilhelmine 1811–81, Ludwig 1813, Catharina Elisabeth 1815–83, Amalie 1818–45 ×Paffe 1837 + recorded at death as Sasse's wife (conflict flagged), Maria 1821–95, Philipp 1r b. 20 Nov 1825, d. 3 Feb 1872 Gemeinde-Vorsteher × Josephine Florentine Schildknecht 1849). Hillebrand children: Andreas (b. 5 Jul 1806, d. 5 May 1883), Joh. Christoph "II" (b. 1 Oct 1808, d. 10 Mar 1860 × Wilhelmine Schrage b. 1812 d. 1871), Anna Catharina (1811), Charlotte (b. 6 Sep 1814, d. 20 Jun 1859, × Heggemann/Kloppmann 1838 — surname discrepancy flagged). Two-wives overlap 1808–14 → possibly two Joh. Christophs; flagged.
  • The "second Wilhelmine geb. Konze" resolved: b. 12 Mar 1820, daughter of Johann Heinrich Konze (1790–1865) × Anna Catharina Köster (d. 25 Oct 1848); × Philipp Köster 2r (1821–1861); d. 6 Mar 1857. Her brother Ludwig b. 31 Oct 1821, d. 30 Apr 1883.
  • Johannes Konze, vormaliger Vorsteher (Trift 66): b. 21 Apr 1771, d. 12 Dec 1838, × Katharina Elisabeth Jordan (d. 1842) — the "Entry 379" couple dated.
  • New Konze wives found: Maria × Christoph Schildknecht (d. pre-1856); Katharina Elisabeth × Hellwig Weisenbach (d. pre-1848); Charlotte × K.W.L. Meimbresse; Anna Katharina (1793–1842) × P.H. Kuhlmann (his ×1 = Maria Wilhelmine Konze 1790–1811).
  • Ludwig 3r × Marie Kuhlmann vom Hesselhof: four child deaths (1854, 1855, 1857 stillborn, 1873). Heinrich × Thiele (Steinweg 136¼): Philipp Ludwig (1868–68) + stillborn 1876. Heinrich × Temme (Haus 94): Maria Catharina (1867–69) + unbapt. daughter 1874. Philipp × Wilhelmine Jordan(?) (136¼): Philipp Ludwig 1872–72.
  • 218824 baptisms: households sorted — Andreas×Loßin, Andreas×Thiele, Joh.Christoph×Köster (Kirchenältester sen., 1740–1817), Joh.Christoph×Hillebrand, Joh.Christoph×Schrage, J.H.×Köster, Joh.Christoph×Temme, Johannes×Hofedick (1824, irregular phrasing); the two ~1824/25 Philipps separated (b. 22 Sep 1824 — parentage LOW-confidence, vs b. 20 Nov 1825 Schrage). E. Trendelburg — generation zero refined; Jungkonze wife corrected

  • TWO senior Andreases (sen./jun. attested 1712–15): Andreas sen., Ratsschöffe, × Margretha Pfankuchen (b. ~1683, d. 25 Sep 1746), d. 1742–46 (own burial missing — anomaly); Andreas jun., Kirchen-Senior (b. ~Dec 1672, d. 21/22 Apr 1761) × A.C. Klieben. Arnold Philipp (bap. 4 Mar 1708) + Mathias (bap. 4 Mar 1714, d. 1 Jun 1742 — "des Sinnes beraubt," mentally disabled, kept at home) assigned to sen. provisionally; Anna Catherina ×2 (1712, 1713) split between the two.
  • Nies Sr.'s burial FOUND: Dionysius Konde, Bürger u. Ackermann, b. ~25 Aug 1681, d. 29 Sep 1746, bur. 1 Oct 1746 (widow + 5 daughters + 1 son). A second Dionysius ("vor dem Thor," b. ~1699, d. 4 Mar 1742). Burials 1719–1736 are MISSING from 220093 (vacancy after Pastor Hausmann).
  • Joan Henric Konde (the baker Kirchen-Senior, 1705–1762): wife ×1 Dorothea Elisabeth geb. Bornemann (d. 24 Apr 1741); infant daughters d. 1742, 1745. Arnold Philipp's infant son Andreas d. 8 Sep 1745. 1741: "Christophs Konden in Deyssel Ehefrau" — earliest Deisel-Konde cross-mention.
  • Maria Christina Konde × David Kersting: penance + copulation 12 Jan 1748 (S.397).
  • Jungkonze: the Fruchtmesser's wife is Martha Elisabetha geb. SCHELLHASE (1785 + 1793 entries; the 1774 "Köhler" reading conflicts — Schellhase preferred, flagged). "Jungkontze Joh. Georg S.52" = Joh. George b. 15 Oct 1785 (the infant d. Jan 1786). 1755 bride's surname settled: SCHNELL (Amtsdiener's daughter; 1756 penance: "Joan George JungKonde von Stammen gebürtig"). Catharina Elisab. Jungconze's baptism is NOT in 220096 1773–1803 (full sweep) → b. pre-1773 or elsewhere; a second Maria Elisabeth Jungkontz daughter attested 1795. Johann Friedrich Kontze had a brother Philip (godfather 1795, "des Vaters Bruder," son of Rathsverwandter Andreas). Joh. Philipp Contze zu Bühne im Paderbornischen (godparent 1797/1800) — a Westphalian Kontze!
