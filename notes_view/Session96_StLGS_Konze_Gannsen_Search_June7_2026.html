Session 96 — StLGS Database Sweep: Konze / Konzen / Gannsen Variants

Date: 7 June 2026 Source: St. Louis Genealogical Society member databases (stlgs.org, logged in) Searches run: surname Konze (beginning — also captures Konzen, Konzelman); surname Gannsen (metaphone — captures Gannson, Gonnson, Gansen, Ganson, Ganzen, Koncen, etc.); surname Kontz (beginning); surname Gons (beginning); surname Gann (beginning); place Kurhess (beginning); place searches Trendelb, Deisel, Hofgeism, Helmarsh (all beginning).

★ HEADLINE FIND (NEW): Wilhelm Konze, 1853 passenger from Kurhessen

Ernst Moritz Arndt Passenger List, Bremerhaven → New Orleans, arrived 30 Nov 1853 (list includes only passengers with destination St. Louis; transcribed by Gerry Meier, StLGS Quarterly vol. 32, no. 4 (1999), pp. 156–158)

Konze, Wilhel. — age 38 — Man — Farmer — home town "Kurkessen" (= Kurhessen)

  • Traveling alone; the only Konze aboard. Implied birth ~1815.
  • Directly relevant to Priority Queue item 6 (passenger lists 1850–1859).
  • The Quarterly Surname Index hit "Konze 32:157" is this same record.
  • URL: https://stlgs.org/research-2/government/immigration/ernst-moritz-arndt-passenger-lists-30-november-1853
  • Follow-up: original NOLA manifest (NARA M259) for this ship/date to verify spelling and check adjacent surnames for Hofgeismar-district neighbors (other passengers from Herford, Hannover, Bielefeld — Wilhelm is an outlier as the lone Kurhessen man).

Not previously in project files (no prior hits for "Brenneke" or "Anton Wilhelm Konze").

Hypothesis to test: passenger Wilhelm Konze (b. ~1815) vs. Anton Wilhelm Konze (d. 1887 age 81 → b. ~1806). Ages conflict by ~9 yrs; could be the same man (loose ages) or kin. Henriette's age (b. ~1842) suggests she may have been a second/much younger wife — or the death-register age is off. Pull St. Paul's Cemetery / church book page images (Page View links) before concluding.

Also: Naturalization — Konze, Louis, 17 Apr 1874, Germany, Circuit Court vol. 10 p. 234, MINOR (minor = arrived <18; b. likely 1850s — possibly a son of the above, or of another Konze line). Hillcrest Abbey: Louis Konze buried 9 Feb 1920.

Confirmations of known evidence (already on site — independent StLGS corroboration)

  • St. Louis Marriages 1804–1876: Charles Gonnson m. Augusta Kreichelt, 7 Jan 1866 — Vol/Page 12-146, SLGS VM5095, MO Archives C6137-range (C6136), LDS film 528178. (= evidence card 3)
  • Death Register: Gannson, Augusta — d. 19 Dec 1896, age 49, b. Germany, 2130 De Kalb St., buried St. Marcus Cemetery. (= evidence card 8 'Auguste Conson'; NOTE — StLGS death-register transcription reads "Gannson", an even closer match to the pension-file spelling than "Conson".) New detail to verify: the 2130 De Kalb St. address.
  • St. Marcus (Old) Cemetery index: Gansen, Augusta — burial 24 Dec 1897 ⚠️ year discrepancy vs. death register (Dec 1896). One of the two transcriptions is off by a year — pull both Page Views.
  • St. Marcus (Old) Cemetery index: Gansen, George — buried 31 Oct 1867, age 1 yr. (already on charles_america/old-st-marcus-cemetery.html)

The Catholic Konzen/Koncen family (Rhineland line — almost certainly NOT ours, now better documented)

  • St. Louis Marriages: Matthaeus Konzen m. Sophia Conrad 22 Nov 1852 (07-266); Mathiaus Konzen m. Mary Schmidt 27 Aug 1872 (16-373); cf. Mathaeus Kunzen m. Barbara Laguire 26 Apr 1860 (10-028) — plausibly the same man, thrice married.
  • 1860 census steamboat workers: Conzen, Mathews, 25, Ward 1, river man (b. ~1835 — age fits Charles's cohort but he persists as Koncen, Catholic, to 1910).
  • St. Louisans with 50 Years Residency in 1909: "Koncen, Mathias — came 1842." Burials at Calvary (Catholic): Mathias d. 5 Oct 1910 age 75; Christian d. 24 May 1872 age 68 b. Germany; descendants (Mathias J, Joseph G, Viola, William Conrad...) at Calvary/Resurrection/Sunset through the 1960s–2000s.
  • Useful as a disambiguation table entry for the Konzen-puzzle page: this is the family that stays Koncen/Konzen and stays Catholic; Charles's line is Protestant (St. Marcus/Evangelical).

Negative results (logged)

  • Place searches Trendelb*, Deisel*, Hofgeism*, Helmarsh*: 0 hits in all StLGS collections.
  • Place Kurhess*: 15 hits, no Konze/Gannsen (Holy Ghost marriages 1844; Zion/St. John's European-origin indexes; naturalizations Peilert/Reifschneider/Waupel; Methodist preachers; Henry Zelch).
  • Johannes passenger list (Bremen → NOLA, 23 Oct 1854, Quarterly 32:3 pp. 111–116): no Konze/Gannsen/Charles candidate; passengers mostly Kreis Herford / Bünde / Bohemia. Only Hesse-adjacent: Carl Huck, 24, Wächtersbach; Ernst Wehner, 21, Philippsthal. These are the only two ship lists StLGS holds.
  • GAR Missouri Death Rolls Index: no Gannsen/Gannson/Gonnson (only Irish Gannan/Gannon). GAR Hassendeubel Post 13 roster: none.
  • Naturalization index: no Charles under Gann/Gons/Konz* variants (closest: "Ganssen, Dude H., Oldenburgh" — different origin; Louis Konze 1874 as above). Charles's naturalization (if any) is not in StLGS's index under these spellings.
  • Kontz (Adam, Concordia Cemetery, d. 1915) — unrelated.

Register-image pulls (added same session)

1. Holy Ghost Evangelical marriage, 3 Jan 1861 (HOLY11-M-1861-1.pdf, p. 1 = register Seite 129, entry 684):

"684. Am 3. Januar. Anton Wilhelm Konze aus St. Louis und Henriette Louise Brenneke aus St. Louis. Zeugen: Friedr. Brenneke & Heinr. Menne."

  • ✗ No German birthplace — register gives residence only ("aus St. Louis") for this period.
  • Witness Friedrich Brenneke → same surname as the 1893 St. Paul UCC marriage (Anna Konze m. Friedrich Brenneke) — Brenneke family double-link.
  • PDF: https://stlgs.org/media/pdf/cong/HolyGhostUCC/HOLY11-M-1861-1.pdf

2. St. Paul UCC death register, 1887 (STPA06-D-1881.pdf, p. 14 = register Seite 115, entry 52):

"52. Anton Konze | gest. 15 Sept. 1887 | begr. 16 Sept. 1887 | Wassersucht [dropsy] | 81 J. 3 Tage | St. Paul [Kirchhof]."

  • Age at death 81 years 3 days → exact birthdate 12 September 1806. This is a precise, searchable date for Archion Kurhessen baptism registers (Sep 1806).
  • ✗ No birthplace column in this register (Nro/Name/Todesdatum/Begräbnis/Krankheit/Alter/Kirchhof only).
  • Implication: if 12 Sep 1806 is accurate, Anton Wilhelm is not the 1853 passenger Wilhelm Konze (b. ~1815 per ship list) unless the ship-list age is badly off. Two distinct Kurhessen-era Konze males remain possible.
  • PDF: https://stlgs.org/media/pdf/cong/StPaulsUCCGilesCity/STPA06-D-1881.pdf (p. 14)

★ THE LEAD: Seite 47 ends ~June 1806, so Seite 48 = second half of 1806. The Taufregister index places "Kontze, Andreas — Seite 48" — i.e., a Kontze boy baptized in Deisel in late 1806, exactly the window for a 12 Sep 1806 birth. Given-name drift Andreas → Anton (Wilhelm) over a 30+-year emigration gap is plausible. Locating and transcribing the Seite 48 Andreas Kontze entry is now the single highest-value target for the Anton Wilhelm question.

Also noted in passing (image 1700, S.46–47, 1806): at least two baptisms with mothers "geb. Kontzin" (Schildknecht and Kontze-Schildknecht couples) — consistent with the known Deisel Kontze-mothers cluster, and a marginal note "1860" on one entry.

Further probing (same session) — the gap is real and Seite 48 is its first page:

⚠ STRUCTURAL FINDING: in the filmed reel, the Deisel Taufen run breaks at Seite 47 (≈June 1806) and resumes at Seite 98 (1815). Seiten 48–97 (≈July 1806–1814) are missing from the film as probed — and "Kontze, Andreas — S.48" (the late-1806 baptism, prime candidate for b. 12 Sep 1806) is on the FIRST missing page. The lone out-of-order leaf at image 1700 (S.46–47 again) confirms leaves were re-shot/misordered; the S.48–97 block may exist elsewhere on the reel (unprobed images 1–1572, 1703–1882) or under KB 218821's own viewer ID.

Gap-hunt completed (continuation, same session) — S.48–97 are NOT on Archion:

  • Image 1560 = duplicate of the 1796 foreword; 1520 = blank leaf (end of preceding book); pre-1573 zone = earlier registers, not the gap.
  • Images 1827–1882 = Begräbnisse series — burials S.46–47/1816 at image 1851 and S.72–73 at 1863 confirm the stored formula image = 1827 + ceil((Seite−1)/2). The "Taufen S.1 1796" leaf at image 1840 is another inserted re-shoot, like image 1700.
  • Viewer 218821 = "Deisel, Langenthal KB 1736–1796" — ends before the gap.
  • Deisel's complete Archion register list (checked): KB 1633–1736, 1736–1796, 1796–1831, 1858–1875; Taufen 1830–1861, 1861–1906, 1907–1979; Konfirmationen 1831+; Trauungen 1830+; Beerdigungen 1830+. No volume other than KB 1796–1831 covers baptisms 1806–1814.

VERDICT: Deisel Taufen Seiten 48–97 (≈Jul 1806–1814), including "Kontze, Andreas — S.48" (prime candidate for Anton Wilhelm Konze b. 12 Sep 1806), are missing from the Archion filming. Only the physical register in Kassel has them.

Route 1 (Archion text search) — DEAD END (checked same session): the viewer's "txt" panel for KB 218824 reports "Bisher keine Texterkennung — Texterkennung könnte beauftragt werden, entsprechendes Kontingent fehlt." No AI transcription exists for this register; commissioning OCR needs a quota (Kontingent) the account lacks — and since Archion's Texterkennung runs on the scans, it could never recover the unfilmed Seiten 48–97 anyway. (The "komplett transkribiert" badge on the parish page evidently does not mean searchable text for this volume.) Login status confirmed active (Mein Konto → Abmelden visible).

Remaining routes: 1. Landeskirchliches Archiv Kassel query (now the only route to Seite 48): cite "Deisel Taufbuch 1796–1831, Seiten 48–97 fehlen in der Archion-Verfilmung (Sprung von S.47/1806 auf S.98/1815); erbitte Scan von Seite 48, Täufling Andreas Kontze (lt. alphabetischem Taufregister, Bild 1887)". ★★★ BREAKTHROUGH: ANTON WILHELM CONZE BAPTISM FOUND — TRENDELBURG, EXACT DATE MATCH ★★★

Register 220096 (Trendelburg KB 1773–1818), image 1059, Seiten 150–151, "Ferner Getaufte 1806", right page, entry [Sept.] 17 (follows Sept. 7 and Sept. 9 entries on the left page; no intervening month header):

"17. Anton Wilhelm, Joh: Christoph Conze, Grenadier unter dem Leib-Grenadier-Bataillon, und Ehefrau Anna Catharina geb. Pfeilin von Homberg, Eheleute; geboren am 12ten Morgens gegen 10 Uhr. Gevatter: Anton Pfeil, Landbereiter(?) zu Carlshaven."

Conclusion: the St. Louis Anton Wilhelm Konze (m. Henriette Brenneke 1861, d. 1887, St. Paul UCC family) is the Anton Wilhelm Conze baptized Trendelburg 17 Sep 1806 — name AND birthdate match exactly. The St. Louis Konze line traces directly to Trendelburg, Kurhessen — the project's prime parish.

Readings verified at max zoom (same session): "Conze" unambiguous; "Leib Grenadier Bataillon"; "Anna Catharina geb. Pfeilin von Homberg"; "gebohren am 12ten Morgens gegen 10 Uhr"; "Gevatter Anton Pfeil Landbereuter zu Carlshaven". Same page also has a Homberg cross-reference (entry 31, Philipp Julius, Martha Elisabeth Dieckhaus von Homberg; Gevatter Valentin Dieckhaus, Bürger zu Homberg) — consistent scribe usage of "Homberg" as a town name (most plausibly Homberg/Efze, the garrison context fits a Hessian grenadier).
