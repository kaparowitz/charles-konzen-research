The Rathsverwandter Andreas Kontze line — the Stammtafel branch

The 1795 marriage names the groom’s father as Rathsverwandter Andreas Kontze of Trendelburg; the earlier reconstruction derived Johann Friedrich from Andreas Kontze I, the Deisel Küster. The two father-identifications conflict and are flagged for resolution (see errata). The Rathsverwandter himself is now exactly dated — and is almost certainly the “Andreas, son of Nies Sr.” who married Anna Christina Konzen in 1760, and the same man as the 1787 godfather “Andreas Kontze the Elder of Stammen.”

The Jungkonze / Jungkontze branch Trendelburg & Stammen

A compound surname (“young Konze”) treated by the registers as a separate but related family, documented from 1755 with explicit Stammen roots. Whether it is a cadet branch of the Kontze line or a distinct family remains unresolved. The whole branch is now exactly dated (gap-closing reads of 6–7 June 2026).
