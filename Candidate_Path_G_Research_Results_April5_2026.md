#### Context — German emigration volume:
- ~20,000 Hessians emigrated to America in 1854 alone; over 9,000 from Hesse-Kassel.
- From 1848–1854, ~773,000 Germans emigrated, with nearly 2/3 from Hesse-Kassel, Hesse-Darmstadt, the Palatinate, Baden, and Württemberg.
- Hamburg passenger lists (1850–1934) contain nearly every emigrant's name, birth date, and departure date. These lists are on Ancestry and FamilySearch microfilm.
- **Le Havre departure lists for 1850–1870 were DESTROYED** — if any Konze son emigrated via Le Havre, those records are lost.

4. **Inge Auerbach's "Hessische Auswanderer" (HesAus)** — published volumes covering Hesse-Kassel emigrants 1840–1850 (Vol. II) and 1850–1866 (Vol. III, possibly unpublished).
