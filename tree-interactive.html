Pan, zoom, and click any person for biographical detail. Auguste's documented ancestors above; Charles & Auguste at the centre; seven children below; key grandchildren on the surviving descendant lines.

An hourglass layout. Charles's German-side ancestors are unknown — they're the project's central open question — and so the upper-left of the tree is intentionally blank. Auguste's parents and grandparents are documented and shown in full. The descendants flow down through the seven children of 1866-1880; both surviving descendant lines (through Erwin Carl and Bertha) are highlighted in gold.

Charles Research — a multi-month transatlantic genealogical investigation into Charles Konzen (recorded in U.S. records as Gannsen / Johnson; 1835–1903), Hessian-born Civil War sergeant of Battery B, 2nd Missouri Light Artillery, and his probable Trendelburg / Deisel origins in Churhessen (a working conclusion — the German baptism remains unrecovered).

      
Compiled by Jed Johnson with Peter Schräder and James Gunnis.
