## 4. ★ Friedrichsfeld follow-up #1 — Lüdicke study → CHECKED, with a real find
Martina Lüdicke, *"Verschwunden ist der Strand …": Studien zur Auswanderung nach Amerika
aus Kurhessen 1831–1866, … Deisel (Kreis Hofgeismar)* (Hofgeismar 1994, 92 pp.) was
reached via **Google Books in-book search**: **"1 page matching Konze — p. 89."**

The p. 89 snippet is the appendix roster **"Auswanderung aus Deisel nach Amerika nach
1876."** Under *ohne Jahresangabe* (no year given) the line reads:

> **KONZE, 2 Brüder** — two Konze brothers, Deisel → USA, date not recorded.

- **First published, name-level record of Konze emigration from Deisel to America** —
  direct support for the Deisel-Konze → US migration the whole Charles hypothesis rests on.
- **But post-1876 → not Charles** (he was in St. Louis from the 1850s); the brothers are
  later kin, plausibly chain migration.
- **Konze appears only on p. 89.** The study's **core 1831–1866 emigrant tables contain no
  Konze** — consistent with Charles emigrating in the 1850s un-indexed / under a changed
  surname.
