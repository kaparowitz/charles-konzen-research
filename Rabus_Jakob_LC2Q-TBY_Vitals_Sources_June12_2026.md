## Update — June 13, 2026: marriage sourced from the original register

**Found the actual marriage entry** in the Donauwörth evangelical marriage register. The earlier tree value "4 June 1584" was wrong; the register and the family group sheet agree on **8 June 1585**.

- **Record:** Evang. Kirchengemeinde Donauwörth, *Eheregister 1579–1629* (Band 15), p. 33, under month header "Junius" 1585, marginal day **8**. Held at Evangelisches Archiv Baden und Württemberg (Auslandsgemeinde Donauwörth); ARCHION digital image 17/102.
- **Permalink:** https://www.archion.de/p/f619a338dd/  (the late-June/July 1584 spread, for reference, is https://www.archion.de/p/22c61382e1/)
- **Transcription:** "Herr M[agister] Jacobus Rabus, [weiland] Herrn M. Jacob Rabus seligen, gewesenen Pfarrers und Superintendenten [zu Monheim], ehelich nachgelassener Sohn; [und] Anna, Herrn [N.] Walther[s] … eheliche Tochter." (Some words faded/abbreviated; brackets = reasoned reading.)
- **Translation:** "Master Jacob Rabus, lawful surviving son of the late Master Jacob Rabus, sometime pastor and superintendent [at Monheim]; and Anna, lawful daughter of Herr [N.] Walther."

## The person (who he is)

Jakob Rabus, b. ~1560/61 Monheim, son of the Monheim superintendent Jakob Rabus (d. 1581). Studied Ulm, Lauingen (Gymnasium Illustre), Tübingen (matric. 5 Mar 1578 as "Monhemensis"; B.A. 24 Sep 1578, M.A. 12 Aug 1580). 1581–1590 deacon at Monheim; **1590–1627 pastor at Walting** (bei Pleinfeld, Lkr. Weißenburg-Gunzenhausen). Expelled when Walting was re-Catholicized 1627/28; family relocated to Protestant Enhofen, parish Ettenstatt. ∞ Donauwörth 4 Jun 1584 Anna Walter.

## 1. Christening — corrected to "about 1561, Monheim"

The old value "Neuburg, Kempten, Schwaben" is an impossible place-string that entered the tree from a 2004 compiled family group sheet (Mirielle Leuzinger-Müller) plus bad place-standardization. He was born/baptized at **Monheim**, where his father was pastor and superintendent 1552–1581.

**Why no original baptism entry exists:** the Monheim parish registers begin only in **1581** (Taufen 1581–1617) — see Matricula Online, Bistum Eichstätt, [Monheim](https://data.matricula-online.eu/de/deutschland/eichstaett/monheim/). Monheim was Protestant until 1617. So a ~1560/61 baptism predates any surviving register; date entered as "about 1561."

**Reason statement saved on the Christening fact** cites: the 1583 Pfalz-Neuburg visitation protocol ("22 years old in 1583," via Kugler papers in Memories); Neuburgisches Pfarrerbuch no. 727; ODP "1560, aus Monheim"; Tübingen matriculation "Monhemensis."

## 2. Death — August 1628 kept; bracketed by original records 1627 × 6 Jan 1631

No church-book entry of his death has been found. The "August 1628" date rests only on the **Kugler/Lifferth research papers** (Memories). The Neuburgisches Pfarrerbuch authors themselves did not know the date (H. Ammon to G. Lifferth, 17 Oct 1958, in Memories).

**What original records establish a death window:**
- Pastor of Walting through 1627; his son Jakob jr. became adjunct there **22 Apr 1627** after expulsion from Natternbach, Austria (Kath. Pfarramt Walting letter, 24 Jul 1958, Memories).
- **Ettenstatt communicant list, Epiphany (6 Jan) 1631:** his wife communes **alone** as *"Anna Rabusin, alte gewes. Pfarrerin zu Walting"* — i.e. styled as the widow/former pastor's-wife of Walting — next to son *"Jacobus Rabus Exul zu Ettenstadt"* (with wife Johanna) and daughter *"Anna Maria Rabusin von Walting."* **Jakob sr. himself appears nowhere** in the surviving Ettenstatt lists 1628–1632 → he was dead by 6 Jan 1631.
  - Archion, Ettenstatt, Taufen/Trauungen/Bestattungen/Abendmahl 1628–1632 (Sig. 9.5.0001-067-02), image 51/76, permalink **https://www.archion.de/p/69bf4634de/**

**Conclusion entered:** died between **22 Apr 1627 and 6 Jan 1631**, traditionally August 1628.
