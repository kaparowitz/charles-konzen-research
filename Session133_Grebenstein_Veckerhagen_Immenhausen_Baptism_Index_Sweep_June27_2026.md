# Session 133 — Grebenstein · Veckerhagen · Immenhausen: full baptism-INDEX sweeps (Archion)

**Date:** 27 June 2026
**Operator note:** request was "sweep the full baptism index window for
brebenstein, veckerhagen, immenhausen." **"Brebenstein" = Grebenstein** (the
three always queue together; there is no Brebenstein parish in the project).

## Objective

These three adjacent Hofgeismar parishes were previously searched only at the
**narrow 1835 birth window** (page-level) and marked ELIMINATED. This session
upgrades them to a **full alphabetical name-index sweep** of each baptism
register — the higher-coverage method already used for Gottsbüren (219049) and
Helmarshausen (219214). The point is to catch *any* Konze-pool surname in *any*
year of the register (e.g. an older or younger sibling), not just a 26-Jan-1835
male. If the Konze family had lived in any of these towns, a same-surname
baptism would surface in the index even outside the 1835 month.

**Surname pool scanned** (the letters where every variant sorts):
- **K** — Konze, Kontze, Konsen, Konzen
- **C** — Conson, Contze
- **G** — Gansen, Gonsen, Gonson, Gannsen, Gans
- **I/J** — Jansen, Jungkonze (J files under I in these Kurrent indexes)

Method: open each register on Archion (subscriber viewer, Jed's login),
navigate to the alphabetical *Register* at the end of the Taufen section,
read the C / G / I-J / K leaves at full-screen hi-res, transcribe every
surname in the Männlich + Weiblich columns by eye, scan for the pool.

## Verdict

All three baptism registers are now searched at **full-index** coverage across
their entire windows (Grebenstein 1830–39, Veckerhagen 1830–51, Immenhausen
1830–45), not merely the 1835 month. **No Konze / Kontze / Konsen / Konzen /
Conson / Contze / Gans / Gansen / Gonson / Gannsen / Jansen / Jungkonze surname
appears in any of the three** — confirming (and strengthening) their prior
ELIMINATED status. The earlier near-misses are unaffected: the Veckerhagen
illegitimate "Wilhelm Walter" (~Mar 1835, surname Walter) stays ruled out; the
Grebenstein 1835 illegitimate birth was female. The surname pools in all three
towns are tight, local Hofgeismar-district families with zero Konze presence.
