# Session 125 — Sielen Baptism-Register Sweep (Archion 220042), 1833–1841
## Date: June 26, 2026

## Objective
Sweep the **Sielen Taufen 1830–1876** register (Archion churchRegister **220042**)
across the **1833–1841** birth window for: (a) **Charles** — a boy named
**Carl/Karl born 26 Jan 1835** who would later anglicise to "Gannsen"; and
(b) **any Konze / Kontze / Gansen / Gonsen / *n·s·n surname** anywhere (child,
parent, or godparent). This is the **baptism track** — Sielen's confirmation
register (220039, Session 112) and the early combined KB burials (220033,
Session 10) were already swept; the 1833–41 baptisms had **not** been done.

Driven live in the logged-in Archion browser (subscription content — in-viewer
workflow, not the web-data plugins).

## RESULT — NEGATIVE for Charles; NO Konze surname anywhere

### The 1835 birth-core leaf (Bild 1844 / Seite 16) — decisive
1835 Jan / Feb / early March holds **five** baptisms: four girls
(Maria Sophie Hofeditz, Louise Amalie Wingert, Maria Sophie [Wintermann],
+ one more) and **one boy** — and that boy is **Nro 79, "Knabe —" with no name
recorded, born 18 Jan 1835, Hausnummer 101** (a stillbirth / died before naming;
non-Konze parents). The only readable boy on the leaf is Nro 83 **Friedrich
Wilhelm Blum**. The two "Karl" tokens the AI flagged on this leaf are **godparents**
(weaver Karl Ritter; Karl Ludwig Winde…). **No boy named Carl, and no boy born
26 Jan 1835.** Charles is **not** baptised in Sielen.
Permalink: **https://www.archion.de/p/700c4c0c76/** (Bild 1844 / Seite 16).

### No Konze/Kontze/Gansen/*n·s·n surname, 1833–1841
Every leaf's full panel text was regex-scanned for Konze-type forms — **zero hits**
across the whole window (children, parents, and godparents). The Sielen surname pool
is the same tight local set throughout: Hofeditz/Haseditz, Baumann, Wassermeier/-meyer,
Frisch, Lück, Heise, Korte, Lenzing, Thöne, Schaub, Helmbrecht, Wiegand, Birber,
Bößler, Rode, Heuermann, Hallfas, Blum, Jordan, Nägeler, Otto, Zwiste, Oderwald, etc.

### Cross-confirmation with the confirmation register (Session 112)
The **Carl Ludwig Bößler** baptism (b. 19 Mar 1839, father Friedrich Wilhelm
Bößler, Leineweber) is the **same boy** as the **1853 confirmand "Carl Ludwig
Bößler, b. 19 März 1839"** found in Sielen Konfirmationen 220039 (Session 112).
Birth date and father's given names + occupation match exactly. NB: the **baptism
AI mis-transcribed the surname as "Köhler"**; the image (underlined surname +
father "Friedrich Wilhelm … Leineweber") confirms **Bößler**, agreeing with the
confirmation record. (Storch and Stein, the other two confirmation-class Carls,
are b. 1840 — they fall just past this leaf-set; consistent with their 1854 class.)

## Reliability notes
- Given names read reliably in the AI panel; the Carl/Konze regex was run over the
  **full** panel text (both the child column and the parents/godparents columns),
  so a Konze *father* or *godparent* would have surfaced even where a child's name
  was illegible.
- Unnamed/stillborn boys in-window (Bild 1839/S11 entry 53, Sept 1833; Bild 1843/S15,
  1834; Bild 1844/S16 Nro 79, b. 18 Jan 1835) were each checked — all "Knabe" with
  no given name, non-Konze parents, not Jan-1835-Carl. Not Charles.
- Wording switched **Knabe/Mädchen → Männlich/Weiblich at Seite 52 (1842)**; the
  late-1841 boys (Friedrich Eduard Wiegand, Heinrich Schaub b.28 Nov, Christian
  Zwiste b.12 Dec) were read via full-text extraction. Year-end tally:
  **"Geboren im Jahr 1841: 21 Personen."** Window closed.
