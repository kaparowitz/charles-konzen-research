## 1. Christening / Baptism — FOUND

**Source:** Evangelisch-Lutherische Kirche in Thüringen, Kirchgemeinde Gotha **St. Margarethen**, Taufen (baptisms) 1845–1848, **page 99, entry No. 58**.
Archion digital image: register 353444, **image 102** (`https://www.archion.de/de/viewer/churchRegister/353444`).

> Note: This is **St. Margarethen**, NOT St. Augustin. Berthold was verified to be absent from the St. Augustin 1847 baptism register (checked both the index and the chronological entries), then located at St. Margarethen. A later-hand death note "† 3 Jan 1908" in the entry confirms the identity.

### Transcription (German)
- **No.:** 58
- **Täufling (child):** Heinrich Friedrich Berthold (recorded in this name order); noted as the 5th child.
- **Vater (father):** Meister Georg Christoph Rabus, Schuhmacher (master shoemaker), hiesig (of this place).
- **Mutter (mother):** Auguste Friederica Therese, geborene Merbach.
- **Geburt (birth):** den 3ten (dritte) April [1847], früh 5 Uhr.
- **Taufe (baptism):** den 18ten (achtzehnten) April [1847], in der Kirche, durch den Amtsdiakonus.
- **Paten (godparents):**
  1. Frau Friederike, geb. Rabus — Ehefrau des Heinr. Friedr. Pflüger;
  2. Frau Elise Mathilde, geb. Rabus — Ehefrau des Metzgermeisters Joh. Heinr. Auerbach;
  3. Frau Bertha Magdalene Rosine, geb. Wenz — Ehefrau des Mstr. Georg Fried. Schaar.
- Later annotation: † 3 Jan 1908.

### Translation (English)
Baptism No. 58. Child: Heinrich Friedrich Berthold (5th child). Father: Master Georg Christoph Rabus, shoemaker, of this place. Mother: Auguste Friederica Therese, née Merbach. Born 3 April 1847 at 5 a.m. Baptized 18 April 1847 in the church by the parish deacon. Godparents: (1) Mrs. Friederike Pflüger née Rabus, wife of Heinrich Friedrich Pflüger; (2) Mrs. Elise Mathilde Auerbach née Rabus, wife of master butcher Joh. Heinrich Auerbach; (3) Mrs. Bertha Magdalene Rosine Schaar née Wenz, wife of Master Georg Friedrich Schaar. Later note: died 3 January 1908.

## 2. Marriage — FOUND

**Source:** Kirchgemeinde **Tröchtelborn** (Kirchenkreis Gotha), Taufen/Trauungen/Beerdigungen 1824–1868, **Trauungen page 55** ("Getraute im Jahr 1868").
Archion digital image: register 353945, **image 60** (`https://www.archion.de/de/viewer/churchRegister/353945`).

> Note: Tröchtelborn (the bride's home parish) is on Archion as its own community. The marriage is recorded there (the groom Rabus is also listed in the marriage name-index, image 78).

### Transcription (German)
- **Bräutigam (groom):** (Heinrich) Friedrich Berthold Rabus, Junggeselle, Schuhmacher zu Gotha, 21½ Jahre; Sohn des Georg Christoph Rabus, Schuhmachermeister hierselbst [Gotha], und dessen verstorbener Ehefrau Auguste Friederike Therese geb. Merbach.
- **Braut (bride):** Jungfrau Hulda Maria Lorenz, 23 Jahre; Tochter des weiland Johann Heinrich Friedrich Lorenz, Anspänner zu Tröchtelborn, und dessen verstorbener Ehefrau Juditha Rebecca geb. Geiersbach.
- **Aufgebot (banns):** zu Gotha (Sonntage Misericordias Domini, Jubilate, Cantate) und zu Tröchtelborn (Jubilate, Cantate, Rogate).
- **Einwilligung (consent):** Vater verstorben; der Stadtrat zu Gotha hat den Trauschein ausgestellt / die vormundschaftliche Genehmigung erteilt, d.d. 19. April 1868.
- **Tag der Copulation (marriage date):** "der siebenzehnte Mai" — **17 May 1868**.

### Translation (English)
Groom: (Heinrich) Friedrich Berthold Rabus, bachelor, shoemaker of Gotha, aged 21½; son of Georg Christoph Rabus, master shoemaker of Gotha, and his late wife Auguste Friederike Therese née Merbach. Bride: Hulda Maria Lorenz, maiden, aged 23; daughter of the late Johann Heinrich Friedrich Lorenz, farmer (Anspänner) of Tröchtelborn, and his late wife Juditha Rebecca née Geiersbach. Banns read at Gotha and Tröchtelborn. Paternal consent (father deceased) issued by the Gotha town council, dated 19 April 1868. Married 17 May 1868.

> ⚠ **Date discrepancy:** FamilySearch currently shows the marriage as **7 May 1868**, but the church record reads **17 May 1868** ("der siebenzehnte Mai"). The banns sequence (ending Sunday Rogate = 17 May 1868) is consistent with the 17th. Worth verifying / correcting on FamilySearch.

## 3. Death / Burial 1908 — NOT AVAILABLE ON ARCHION

Berthold died 3 January 1908 in Gotha. **No 1908 burial record could be retrieved from Archion**: the digitized burial registers (Beerdigungen) for **all** Gotha town parishes on Archion end at **1875** —
- Gotha St. Margarethen: Beerdigungen to 1872–1875;
- Gotha St. Augustin: Beerdigungen to 1872–1875;
- St. Augustin [Nord] and [Süd]: only Konfirmanden are digitized after 1876.

Post-1875 Gotha burial books are not on Archion (they remain with the parish/regional archive; civil death registration via the Standesamt Gotha would also exist from 1876). The death is, however, already evidenced by the **"† 3 Jan 1908" annotation** in Berthold's St. Margarethen baptism entry and by the existing "Rabus Family Grave" source on FamilySearch.
