## ★ FS CONFIRMS the Büngner correction (independent of our registers)
FS keeps **two distinct Johann Georg Jungkontze men**, exactly as our 8 Jun correction concluded:
- **Amtsdiener line** — Johann George Jungkonze **MG26-FLC** × Anna Elisabeth Kohlstadt (=Kohlstätten, MG26-FLG); only child on FS = Johann Heinrich (1766, MG26-F2F). No dates on FS (we have b.~1733, d. 21 Feb 1797). Does NOT carry the Büngner sons. ✓
- **Büngner line** — **Johann Georg Jungkontze KZ9R-G91** (chr. **28 Mar 1738**, d. **17 Nov 1792**) × **Elisabeth Büngner** (K8ML-X16), m. **22 May 1767** → son **Joh. Christoph Jungkontze K8KL-8JQ** (chr. **16 Feb 1768**, d. **16 May 1824**) × **Anna Elisabeth Korff** (KHVW-NKK), m. **27 Jan 1799**. FS even gives the prior generation: **Georg Jungkontze K8RM-KXP × Anna Margarethe Regenbogen KZP3-RW5** (1703–1743), m. **24 Mar 1734**.
⇒ Our register-derived parentage (brothers = sons of Joh. George × Elisabeth Büngner, NOT the Amtsdiener) is independently corroborated by the FS tree, which adds exact dates (1738–1792 for the father; 16 Feb 1768 for Joh. Christoph — we had only "~Mar 1768" from age-at-death).

## ✅ DISCREPANCY A — RESOLVED 11 Jun 2026 (FS was right)
- **Register-confirmed (220063 img 1990, Taufen 1808, HTR):** *"den 26. [Juni] Johannes, **Joh. Christoph Jungkontze** und deßen Ehefrau Anna Elisabeth geb. [Korff] Söhnlein, gebohren den 22ten Juny … [margin 1865]. Gevatter ist **Johannes Jungkontze, des Vaters Bruder**."*
- ⇒ **Johannes b. 22 Jun 1808 (†1865) is the son of Joh. Christoph Jungkontze × Korff (= FS KZS8-CMJ), NOT of the Leineweber × Lohr.** The Leineweber Johannes is only the **godfather/uncle** ("des Vaters Bruder"). **Our konze-stammen.html was wrong; corrected 11 Jun 2026** (removed from the Leineweber's children; the "Johannes jr." row re-parented to Joh. Christoph × Korff, with FS marriage × Katharina Kuncke 1835). FS's tree was accurate here.

## ⚠ DISCREPANCY B — place: HÜMME (FS) vs STAMMEN/TRENDELBURG (our registers)
- FS labels the **entire** Joh. Georg(1738–92)×Büngner → Joh. Christoph(1768)×Korff line as **Hümme** (christenings, both marriages, all events).
- Our **primary-source** register reads place key events elsewhere: Johannes the Leineweber's baptism (13 May 1769) in **Stammen KB 1720–1777** (220060 img 1861); Anna Gertrudt's baptism (1775) in **Stammen** (220063 img 1950); Joh. Christoph's marriage (1799) in **Trendelburg** (220096 img 2072).
- FS is unsourced; most likely the tree compiler defaulted the family to Hümme (or Hümme is the family's earlier origin parish and they were at Stammen/Trendelburg by the 1760s–90s). **Reconcile against the registers** — but trust the sourced Stammen/Trendelburg reads for those specific events.
