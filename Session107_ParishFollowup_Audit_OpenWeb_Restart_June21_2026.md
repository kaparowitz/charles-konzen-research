## 5. ADDENDUM — NARA AAD "Germans to America" passenger search EXECUTED (21 June 2026)

The free open-web channel for *Germans to America* turned out to be the **U.S.
National Archives AAD database** (FamilySearch indexes the same data behind a
login; German Roots confirms both). archive.org's full-text route was unusable
this session (repeated 180-s timeouts); the vdoc.pub copy held only a fragment.
So the search was run directly in AAD.

**Search run:** AAD → Genealogy/Immigrants → *German, French, and Swiss Passenger
Records, ca. 1820–1912* (file unit dt=3160; 4,833,869 records). LAST NAME = **any
of** `Konze Kontze Konzen Kontzen Contze Contzen Konsen Gansen Gonsen Gannsen`.
**Result: 81 passenger records.** Every plausible candidate was opened (passenger
detail) and its **Manifest Header** pulled for the arrival date/ship.

**Candidates tested and ELIMINATED (the only ones fitting Charles's profile by name):**

| Passenger | Manifest | Age | Occ. | Origin | Ship · port · **arrival** | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **KONZE, Chas.** (+ Mary) | 36467 | 17 | farmer | Prussia | Main · Bremen→NY · **6 Mar 1882** | ✗ b.~1865; 1882; Prussia |
| **KONZE, Justus** (+ Hanchen, Herrmann) | 45742 | 32 | farmer | — | Broome · Bremen→NY · **8 Sep 1845** | ✗ b.~1813; 1845 ≠ Justus Ernst (b.1829) |
| **KONZE, Christoph** | 10594 | 25 | baker | Prussia | Elise & Mathilde · Bremen→NY · **9 Jun 1862** | ✗ b.~1837, Prussia ≠ Hümme Christoph (b.1814) |
| **GANSEN, Carl** | 8278 | 22 | **doctor** | — | (manifest 8278) | ✗ occupation doctor ≠ Charles the farmer/soldier |

**Pattern in the data:** the **KONZE** surname pool in this index reads as
**Prussian** (Chas. and Christoph both stamped "Prussia"), not Hessian; the
**GANSEN** pool is a **Rhineland-Catholic** family group (given names Mathias,
Nicolaus, Anton, Peter, Joseph, Jacob — manifests 30454, 45914, 7244, etc.), not
Hessian-Lutheran. Two "VON-CONTZEN" (manifest 6451) are US-born, not immigrants.

**Conclusion — NEGATIVE, and it closes the "1859 passenger manifest via the free
NARA index" follow-up.** No passenger in the NARA AAD *Germans to America* index
matches Charles — a Hessian-Lutheran **farmer, b. 26 Jan 1835, arriving ~1859,
bound for St. Louis**. No "Carl/Karl/Charles Konze/Kontze" of the right age,
origin, and date exists in the index at all. This is consistent with the project's
working hypothesis that Charles most likely emigrated **without registering** (the
illegal-emigration angle of the Marburg letter) and/or is **not cleanly indexed**.

**Caveats (per project standard):** AAD/*Germans to America* has known omissions
(only ships flagged with German passengers; some Baltimore arrivals missing;
village-of-origin almost always "unknown"); it is surname-driven, so a badly
mis-transcribed spelling would be missed. The same data underlies the FamilySearch
"Germans to America Index" — a FamilySearch pass would likely return the same 81.
None of the four candidates was verified against the original manifest image
(would require the NARA microfilm / Ancestry image), but age + date + occupation
already exclude each.

**Net new leads worth keeping (not Charles, but additive):** an Arcinsys catalog
entry for *"Wohnhaus und Stall von **Gottlieb Konze zu Deisel**"* (§3.A) — checked
against the project's own records and **reconciled** (see §6): Gottlieb Konze is
*already documented*, so this is a new corroborating **source**, not a new person.

## 6. Loose end tied — "Gottlieb Konze zu Deisel" Arcinsys plan = known man, new source (21 June 2026)

Opened the Arcinsys catalog record in full:

- **Signature:** HStAM, Karten, **P II 9658/3** (Hessisches Staatsarchiv Marburg,
  Fonds Karten → Orte → Deisel (Hofgeismar)). Former id C 0189 a–f.
- **Title:** *Bauaufnahme mit Grundriss und Aufriss für ein Wohnhaus und Stall von
  Gottlieb Konze zu Deisel.* Original title: *"Riss zu einem Wohnhause nebst
  Stallgebäude für Gottlieb Konze in Deisel."*
- **Date:** **1871.** Content: private architecture (a house + stable plan).
  Digitised 2016–21 in the DFG Bildindex/DDK project; no image shown on the Arcinsys
  page itself (deep description via the DDK Bildindex).

**Reconciliation — he is NOT a new person; already in the project records.** A
Gottlieb Konze of Deisel is documented in earlier sessions:
- **Session 27** — *Gottlieb Konze*, **son of Johann Christoph Konze (Ackermann)**,
  confirmed **1831** at Deisel (Reg. 218836, Seite 1) → b. c. **1817**. (Session 27
  also lists a younger *Gottlieb Konze, b. ~1842*, in the marriage register Entry 264
  as a son in the same Johann-Christoph household — see "to reconcile" below.)
- **Session 44** — burial scan of Reg. 218854 identifies a **Gottlieb Konze,
  Gastwirth** (innkeeper, of Trendelburg/Deisel), among the Deisel Konze patriarchs.

So the Arcinsys **1871** building plan (HStAM Karten P II 9658/3) is a **new
corroborating source** for an already-known man: it documents Gottlieb Konze as a
property-owning Deisel householder erecting a house + stable in 1871 — consistent
with the Ackermann/Gastwirth standing already on file. Not Charles, not a new name.

**RESOLVED on Archion (21 June 2026).** Read the Deisel burial register **Reg.
218854, Image 109, Entry 669** (year 1904) at zoom: *"**Gottlieb Konze, Gastwirth**,
Ehemann der Elise geb. [Ege…], **Nº 62½** … geboren **Deisel den 23. Mai 1842**,
gestorben 1. Dezember 1904, beerdigt 4. Dezember 1904."* So the innkeeper Gottlieb
Konze was **born in Deisel on 23 May 1842** — i.e., the **younger** of the two
documented Gottliebs (son of **Johann Christoph Konze**, the one logged in Session 27
at marriage Entry 264), **not** the Gottlieb b. ~1817 (confirmed 1831).

**Conclusion:** the Arcinsys **1871** plan *"Wohnhaus und Stall für Gottlieb Konze zu
Deisel"* (HStAM Karten P II 9658/3) is **Gottlieb Konze the Gastwirth, b. 23 May 1842
in Deisel** — at age **29** building his **inn premises** (a Gasthaus is precisely a
*Wohnhaus + Stall*), house **Nº 62½** in Deisel, where he died in 1904. High
confidence; the only un-closed cross-check is whether the plan's parcel = house 62½
(the catalog entry gives no house number). Note: Session 44's note that this Gottlieb
was *"from Trendelburg"* is corrected here — the burial entry gives his birth as
**Deisel 1842**.

**Filed against the existing Gottlieb-Konze-b.1842 node; loose end closed; no new
tree node created.**
