# Session 130 — Fulda (Stadt) EV baptism index swept for the Konze surname pool, 1833–41

**Date:** 27 June 2026
**Register:** Archion reg. **168326** — Fulda (Stadt) ev. **Taufen 1830–1863**
(Landeskirchliches Archiv Kurhessen-Waldeck → Kirchenkreis Fulda → Fulda; 1091 images).
**Task:** sweep the alphabetical baptism index for the 1833–41 birth window for the
Charles surname pool (Konze/Kontze/Konsen/Konzen · Gansen/Gonsen/Gonson/Gonnson/Gannsen/Gans ·
Conson · Jansen/Jungkonze).
**Verdict:** **NEGATIVE.** No surname-pool baptism anywhere in the Fulda EV register
1830–1863 — hence none in the 1833–41 window. Fulda EV stays **ELIMINATED** (corroborates
Session 127; the Gastmann garrison family remains the only pool-*adjacent* presence and is
already excluded).

---

## 1. Why this sweep

Session 127 verified James Gunnis's flagged **Gastmann** baptism (1838, Bild 852) and
swept a couple of confirmation classes + the garrison parishes — all keyed to that *one*
entry. What had **not** been done was reading the Fulda EV **baptism index itself** for the
Konze pool. This sweep closes that gap: it tests whether *any* Konze/Gansen-type child was
baptised at the Fulda town church across the whole 1830–1863 run, not just whether the
Gastmann boy is Charles. Stronger elimination as a result.

## 5. The two anomalies — both resolved

1. **"Komse 63"** — a garbled token the OCR dropped into the H block on Bild 1075. Checked
   directly: **Seite 63 (Bild 851) = Oct–Dec 1837** (Nro 291–294 = Frieß/Schmidt, **Giebel**
   [Sergeant], Henninghausen, **Lange** [Compagnie-Chirurg]; year-end tally "38 Kinder …
   Fulda 31. Decbr. 1837"). **No Konze/Komse surname** — pure OCR artifact. (Note the
   recurring military families — Sergeant Giebel, Feldwebel Gasche — same garrison pattern
   as Gastmann.)
2. **"Kunze, Wilhelmine Ernestine Friederike Josephina, Seite 100"** (K-continuation,
   Bild 1083) — the single closest K-surname to the pool. **Female**, and Seite 100 falls
   **≈1843–44** (bracketed by Seite 66 = 1838 and Seite 137 = 1849) ⇒ **outside the 1833–41
   window** and the wrong sex for Charles regardless. Logged as a near-name non-match;
   "Kunze" ≠ the Konze/Kontze pool spelling. (Cf. the separate Kunze-OSB crosslink work,
   Session 127.)

## 6. Bottom line

The Fulda (Stadt) EV baptism register 1830–1863 contains **no Konze-pool surname at all** in
its complete alphabetical index — therefore none in the 1833–41 birth window. Combined with
Session 127 (Gastmann baptism + confirmations + garrison-parish sweep), **Fulda EV is fully
eliminated on the baptism track**: the Dassen→Fulda toponym lead produced exactly one
pool-adjacent family (the Gastmann garrison household), already excluded as Charles on
surname + Kreis + birth-year.
