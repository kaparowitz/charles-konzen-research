## Significant Collateral Findings in Hümme Register

### Finding 1 — Johannes Kontze × Marie Magdalene Apfelmann (Entry 8, 1831 May)
**IMPORTANT Konze-Hümme-Deisel connection**

Groom: **Johannes Kontze**, Ackermann, from Hümme
- Father (deceased): **Johann Christoph Kontze**, Ackermann
- Mother (surviving): **Anne Christine geb. Göbel, von Deisel**
- Groom's age: ~30 years (born ~1800–1801)

Bride: **Marie Magdalene Apfelmann**, from Hümme
- Father: Johann Christoph Apfelmann
- Mother: Anne Elisabeth geb. Lichtefeld

**Significance**: This Johannes Kontze is NOT Joh. Heinrich Konze (different given name). But his family originated in Deisel. His father Johann Christoph Kontze is now deceased by 1831. His mother Anne Christine geb. Göbel was from Deisel. This may represent the Kontze family branch that migrated from Deisel to Hümme.

### Finding 2 — Marie Elisabeth Altmann, mother geb. Kontze (Entry 6, 1831 Jan)
Bride in Entry 6: Marie Elisabeth Altmann
- Mother: **Anne Catharine geb. Kontze**
- This confirms a Kontze woman in Hümme who had married an Altmann

### Finding 3 — Multiple Deisel family origins in Hümme
- Entry 7 groom Friedrich Apfelmann: mother **"geb. Schildknecht, von Deisel"**
- Entry 30 groom Johann Heinrich Apfelmann: mother **"geb. Thiele von Deisel"**
- Entry 8 groom Johannes Kontze: mother **"geb. Göbel, von Deisel"**

The Hümme community appears to have substantial Deisel-origin family ties, with Kontze, Apfelmann, Schildknecht, Thiele, and Göbel families all having Deisel roots.
