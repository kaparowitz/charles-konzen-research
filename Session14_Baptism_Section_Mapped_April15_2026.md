### Confirmed Konze-family sightings in 1828 baptism pages

At subscriber-tier zoom on image 1739:

- **Entry 4 (top of 1828)**: Child "Gottlieb" born to father (name obscured by damage) **"et uxoris Maria geb. Konze"** — the wife/mother is Maria née Konze. This does not confirm or falsify Maria Carolina Konze's own baptism but confirms a Konze-born woman was baptising a child in Deisel in 1828.
- **Entry 16 right page**: "Sophia Amalia" with godparent "Johannes Konzen, der Eltern" (possibly "des Vaters Bruder" or similar relation formula) — another godparent sighting of the Konze surname.
- **Image 1745 (option 96)**: "Einwohner Conrad Johannes" visible — a Konrad/Conrad is an Einwohner (resident) of Deisel, possibly named Johannes Conrad (rather than surname Konze).
- **Image 1747 (option 98)**: "Konzen et uxoris" phrase visible — a Konze husband + wife being named in the entry.

| 228 | 1607 | 1754 confirmations (Session 13 finding) |
| 255 | 1633 | 1755 burials (Session 13 finding) |
| 265 | 1644 | Terminal alphabetical index (Session 13 finding) |

### Implication

Register 218821 is NOT a simple chronological baptism-marriage-burial register. It contains **multiple overlapping and interleaved sub-registers**: there appear to be Latin-language entries alongside German, a separate memorial book, at least two confirmation sections, and a clear section break at option 190 (image 1569) that begins a fresh sub-register with its own table of contents. The **1795 baptism section for Andreas Konze must sit somewhere in options 0–100 or between 60–90**, but the precise location has not been established. A targeted search at options 20, 40, 60, 80 is the top Session 15 priority.
