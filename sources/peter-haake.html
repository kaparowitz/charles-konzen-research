Haake / Haacke — Peter Schräder's Family Chart

Hermannus Haacke family · Trendelburg / Deisel / Wülmersen · with linked Gans line in Lippoldsberg
SECONDARY SOURCE Peter chart · PDF

About this chart

Peter Schräder's family-overview chart for the Haacke / Haake family of Trendelburg, Deisel, and Wülmersen, including the marriage of Johanne Caroline Haacke to Johann Daniel Gans of Lippoldsberg in 1829 and their four Gans children — relevant for the Gans / Konze surname-drift line of inquiry.

Why this chart matters

The Gans-Lippoldsberg branch produced four children in Deisel and Lippoldsberg in the 1830s — directly overlapping Charles's hypothesised birth year of 1835. Friedrich Wilhelm Gans, born 24 June 1835, has a near-overlap birth date with the project's leading-hypothesis Konze candidate. The Haake chart documents the family backbone for this comparison.
