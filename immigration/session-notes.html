★ At a Glance

Five session notes from the April 2026 immigration-investigation arc, plus one standing master document. The session work concentrated on the Hildebrand-cohort lead (Sessions 75–77), the Carl Kinzer/Krieger 1859 Liverpool manifest investigation (Session 78), and the FamilySearch Charles Johnson Sr. profile cataloguing (Session 69). Each linked note captures the original session's findings, screenshots, and follow-up actions.

Charles Research — a multi-month transatlantic genealogical investigation into Charles Konzen (recorded in U.S. records as Gannsen / Johnson; 1835–1903), Hessian-born Civil War sergeant of Battery B, 2nd Missouri Light Artillery, and his probable Trendelburg / Deisel origins in Churhessen (a working conclusion — the German baptism remains unrecovered).

      
Compiled by Jed Johnson with Peter Schräder and James Gunnis.
