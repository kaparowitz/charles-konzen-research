## Move 1 — Register 218824 images 1863–1868 (intended: 1828–1829 baptisms)

**Finding:** This entire image range is the **burials section**, not baptisms.

- Image 1863: two-page spread of burial entries with age notations ("alt 22 Jahr", "53 Jahren 2 Monat 3 Tagen" style), clearly burials of an earlier year (1825 or end-of).
- Image 1864 (**critical anchor**): left page, roughly lower-third, contains the clear year header "**Begraben im Jahren 1826**" — confirming this is the opening of the 1826 burials sub-section.
- Image 1867 right page mid-range shows "§ 28 Maria Caroline" style entries, but these are also burial-format lines with age/death-date notation, not baptisms.

**Consequence:** Session 12's mapping — "image 1866 = 1827 baptisms" and "1830 baptisms at image 1872" — must be reinterpreted as **burials** of those years. The register is not organized year-by-year with baptisms-marriages-burials grouped; rather, baptisms, marriages, and burials are each full-register sections spanning 1796–1831. The 1828–1829 baptism pages for Justus Ernst Konze and Maria Carolina Konze are therefore located **elsewhere in the register**, most likely in an earlier-image baptism section (roughly options 0–100, images 1649–1749) not sampled in Sessions 11–12.

## Move 2 — Register 218833 image 1247 entry 217 marginalia (RESOLVED)

**Method:** Subscriber-tier zoom executed via `jQuery('a.zoom-in').click()` fifteen times to drive the Archion tile viewer to maximum magnification, then panned via `.zoom-holderInner` top/left CSS coordinates to bring the lower-left quadrant of the left page into frame.

**Corrected entry 217 transcription** (Konze, Anna Catharina):
```
217) Konze, a. Cath., Joh Hofhoff (B) T. (Köste, b.d.h. Pets.)
     × 31.10.1793  ∞ 3.4.1814 — Kodemann Heinrich
     R. 1808  (× 8.8.1781 2ter Ehe; † d. 13.12.1868)
     † 3.1.1842
```

**The marginalia parenthetical, character-by-character:**
- "× 8.8.1781" = husband Heinrich Kodemann was born 8 Aug 1781 (NOT 28.8.1781 as previously read; the leading "2" belongs to the following abbreviation)
- "**2ter Ehe**" = German abbreviation for *zweiter Ehe* = "from/in [his] second marriage" (this is a marital-order annotation, not a place abbreviation)
- "† d. 13.12.1868" = died on 13 Dec 1868 (d. = *den*, the date connector)

**The "Zeyhl" hypothesis is retracted.** The character group that Sessions 10–12 tentatively read as "Zeyhl" is in fact the "2ter" marriage-order abbreviation. There is no place annotation on this marginalia. The Session 10 "von Zeyhl" thread for Charles Gannsen's origin-place has no support from entry 217 and should be closed unless other evidence surfaces.

**Genealogical implication:** Heinrich Kodemann was 12.7 years older than Anna Catharina Konze, married her on 3 Apr 1814 when he was 32 and she was 20, had been previously married before 1814 (first wife unknown, not yet located), and outlived Anna Catharina (who died 3 Jan 1842) by 26 years, dying 13 Dec 1868. The Kodemann surname is a new research thread: in Deisel or nearby, a Heinrich Kodemann born 8 Aug 1781 with a prior marriage should be traceable.

## Move 1a — Additional Konze entries discovered in Register 218833 K-section

Two Konze entries previously missed in Session 12's re-transcription became visible at higher zoom and under panning:

**Entry 196** (image 1247 left page, upper-middle):
```
196) Konze, Conrad, Andreas [Richter?] T. ([Duthem a.h.a.s.])
     × 16.10.1780
     R. 1794
     † 22.12.1841
```
Conrad Konze, son of Andreas Konze, born 16 Oct 1780 Deisel, died 22 Dec 1841. This places another Andreas Konze (the father) active in Deisel in the 1770s–1780s, likely distinct from the Andreas of entry 114 (which Session 12 corrected to Köster).

**Entry 212** (image 1247 right page, upper):
```
212) Konze, Maria Magdalena, Joh Hofhoff (B) T. ([Heimer a. vergn.])
     × 31.12.1791  ∞ 3.11.1818 — [Helchmanr?] Heinrich
     R. 1806
```
Maria Magdalena Konze, daughter of Joh. Hofhoff Konze (same father as entries 177 Johannes, 202 Joh. Hofhoff, and 217 Anna Catharina), born 31 Dec 1791 Deisel, confirmed 1806, married 3 Nov 1818.

**Revised Deisel Konze family tree from Register 218833 K-section:**

The "Joh. Hofhoff" Konze (entry 117 / Kuntz Joh. Christoph) appears to have fathered **four Konze children** named in the Registerband:
- Johannes Konze, × 21.4.1771, † 14.12.1838 (entry 177)
- **Joh. Hofhoff Konze** (entry 202), × 6.8.1784, ∞ 7.4.1808, † 24.4.1846 — possibly named after his father
- Maria Magdalena Konze, × 31.12.1791, ∞ 3.11.1818 (entry 212)
- Anna Catharina Konze, × 31.10.1793, ∞ 3.4.1814 Kodemann Heinrich, † 3.1.1842 (entry 217)

And **Andreas Konze** (distinct from the father line) fathered Conrad Konze × 16.10.1780 † 22.12.1841 (entry 196).

The "Hofhoff" token is now confidently understood as a **Hausname / farmstead designation** ("of the Hofhof[f] farmstead"), not a surname. Multiple entries write "Joh. Hofhoff" where Joh. is the baptismal name and Hofhoff is the farmstead/by-name.
