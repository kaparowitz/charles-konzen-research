## A. ★ Correction — Christian Ludwig Kontze's couple did NOT leave Stammen after 1835
The Stammen burial book (220072) settles it:
- **Anne Gertrud Kontze geb. Temme** — b. **18 May 1801** Stammen; **d. 31 Jan 1869** (9 p.m.), bur. 4 Feb 1869, **Stammen Haus Nr. 21**; entry: "Anna Gertrude Kontze geborne Temme, **Ehefrau** des Taglöhner Christian Ludwig Kontze." (220072 S.109, E515, img 937.)
- "Ehefrau" (wife, not widow) ⇒ **Christian Ludwig was alive and resident at Haus Nr. 21 in Jan 1869** — the couple stayed in Stammen the whole time (the earlier "left after 1835" inference is wrong). In ~36 years of marriage their ONLY recorded child remains the March-1835 stillborn.
- He died **11 Apr 1883** (baptism margin) — but is **absent from the entire Stammen burial register 1830–1936** (chronological 1883 read + alphabetical K-index). So he died/was buried elsewhere (civil registration at Standesamt, or a neighbouring parish, after 1869). The Stammen K-index does list other later Kontze burials (a stillborn S.15; Johann S.114; Joh. Georg S.125; Gustav Ludwig S.143; Karl Louis S.169; Friedrich S.213) — an ongoing Stammen Kontze presence, separate thread.
