# Charles Gannsen / Kontzen — Prioritized Research Plan (April 14, 2026)

**Objective.** Identify the definitive Hessian birth record and family-of-origin linkage for **Charles L. Gannsen / Johnson** (b. 26 Jan 1835, Kurhessen; d. 1903, St. Louis, MO; "aus Deisse" per 1866 marriage record; surname migration *Kontze → Gonnson → Gannsen → Johnson*).

**Current leading hypothesis.** Charles = **Justus Ernst Konze** (b. 14 Nov 1829, Deisel; son of Andreas Konze b.1795 × Maria Sophia Hofeditz), last documented at Pfingsten 1843 confirmation in Deisel, then vanishes from Hessian records.

Alternatives still live: (2) illegitimate son of Andreas b.1795 born ~1835; (3) unrecorded legitimate child of Andreas b.1795 × Maria Sophia born before Oct 1835.

Mainz scenario is **eliminated** (comprehensive civil-registry search returned zero Konze entries across ~900k Mainz records). Heinrich Wilhelm Kontze (d.1858 Stammen) and Ludwig Konze (d.1875 Deisel) are eliminated.

---

## Tier 1 — Highest-value unsearched sources (do these first)

### P1. Friedrichsfeld Kirchenbuch 1830–2011 (Archion Register 218959), baptisms 1830–1836
The single highest-value unsearched Hessian parish source. Friedrichsfeld sits in the Trendelburg-Hofgeismar corridor and covers the exact baptism window (1835). Anchor at image 1367 and work backward through the 1830–1836 baptism entries. Search for: Konze / Kontze / Contzen / Gans / Gansen / any child of Andreas b.1795 × Maria Sophia Hofeditz, and for an illegitimate "Carl" / "Karl" / "Charles" born 26 Jan 1835 to any Konze-line woman.

### P2. Deisel Kirchenbuch-Registerband 1635–1796 (Archion Register 218833) — K-section lookup
Consolidated alphabetical index for the entire Deisel parish. A 5-minute K-section scan under Kontze / Konze / Contzen resolves whether Andreas Sr. "von Zeyhl" (1745 godfather entry) ever appears as Deisel filial. **Requires Archion subscriber access.**

### P3. Deisel Kirchenbuch 1796–1831 (Archion Register 218824) and 1831+ successor volume
Critical for tracking the Andreas b.1795 household's later life: Justus Ernst's confirmation (Pfingsten 1843 already captured), any later birth of a "Carl" to the same household, and the 1854 Mainz death record of Andreas b.1795 reflected back as a Deisel death-report entry.

### P4. Track Justus Ernst Konze post-June 1843 — emigration records
Having confirmed he disappears from Deisel after Pfingsten 1843 (age 13), the next link is outbound:
- **Hamburg passenger lists** (Staatsarchiv Hamburg, 1850–1859) — search Konze / Kontze / Gannsen / Johnson, age range 20–30.
- **Bremen passenger lists** (reconstructed Bremen manifests via Bremerhaven Auswandererhaus and Die Maus database).
- **LAGIS Hessian emigration database** (Hessisches Staatsarchiv Marburg auswanderer) — Kurhessen official emigration permits.
- **HETRINA** (Hessische Truppen in Amerika) — if he was conscripted.
- **U.S. side:** Castle Garden (pre-1892) and Ellis Island databases under all name variants; St. Louis 1860/1870/1880 federal census for Konze / Gannsen / Johnson aged ~25/35/45 born Hessen.

### P5. Archion Register 220096 (Trendelburg successor KB 1772–1827) — Kontze baptism search 1807–1818
Close the remaining gap on possible male children of Andreas Kontze (b.1787, Trendelburg) × Wilhelmine Grossbernd. Any Kontze son born 1807–1818 becomes a collateral candidate whose own 1830s children could be Charles.

## Tier 3 — Gazetteer / collateral / archival-request leads

### P10. "Zeyhl" place-name gazetteer work
Cross-check **Hessisches Historisches Ortslexikon (HHO)**, **GOV Geschichtliches Ortsverzeichnis**, and **Meyers Orts- und Verkehrs-Lexikon des Deutschen Reichs** for any 18th-century Hessian place spelled *Zehl, Zeyl, Zeyhl, Zeyhlbach, Zellhausen* within 20 km of Trendelburg.

### P11. Heisebeck Kirchenbuch 1820–1850 — archive request
Small Trendelburg-adjacent parish, not on Archion. Request via **Peter Schrader** (local Trendelburg genealogist) or direct **Landeskirchliches Archiv Kassel** inquiry. Check for any Kontze-line 1835 baptism and for the Hofeditz collateral network.

### P12. Hofgeismar Konsistorium / Superintendentur correspondence
The Joh. Georg Jungkontze × Wegner *Consistorial-Rescript* case (Trendelburg KB p.398) was escalated to superintendency. Archive holdings at Landeskirchliches Archiv may contain the originating correspondence and name the child born of that union — a collateral 1760s–70s Jungkontze heir who could provide the missing bridge generation.

### P13. St. Louis, MO local records for Charles L. Gannsen / Johnson
- **1866 marriage record** — already cited "aus Deisse." Pull full original and transcribe every witness signature and birthplace field.
- **1903 death certificate** — parents' names, birthplace, immigration year.
- **Probate / estate file** — heirs and any reference to Hessian correspondents.
- **Church affiliation** (German Evangelical congregations of 1860s–90s St. Louis) — Holy Ghost Evangelical, St. Peter's, etc. — Taufbuch records often list the emigrant's Heimatort explicitly.
- **Naturalization petition** — standard format names village of origin.

### P14. DNA triangulation pass
Re-run Jed Johnson's autosomal DNA through MyHeritage, Ancestry, and GEDmatch with a targeted cluster search for known descendants of: (a) Andreas Konze b.1795 via surviving sisters Maria Carolina (b.1828) and Maria Sabine (b.1834); (b) Trendelburg Kontze Gen 0 Johann Henrich. A single 2nd–4th-cousin hit to a documented Deisel or Trendelburg Kontze descendant would collapse the search.
