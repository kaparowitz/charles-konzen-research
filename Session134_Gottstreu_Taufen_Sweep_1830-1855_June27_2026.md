Lippoldsberg's immediate ring (Oedelsheim, Gieselwerder, Vernawahlshausen, Bodenfelde,

## 1. Parish identification (open web)

- **Wülmersen** is a manor estate (Gut/Wasserschloss Wülmersen) on the NW edge of the
  Reinhardswald — **3.9 km N of Trendelburg, 2.3 km NE of Deisel**. It is *not* a Weser/
  Lippoldsberg neighbour; its parish church is **Deisel (Kirchspiel Trendelburg)** — the
  project's most heavily-searched core parishes (the three "geb. Konzen" mothers are in
  Deisel). So Wülmersen's records already sit inside the Deisel/Trendelburg sweeps; **no
  separate sweep needed.**
- **Gottstreu** is a **1722 Waldensian colony** village (twin of Gewissenruh) in the
  **Kirchspiel Lippoldsberg** (Ev. Kirche von Kurhessen-Waldeck, Kirchenkreis Hofgeismar-
  Wolfhagen). Sources: gemeinde-wesertal.de, kirchenkreis-hofgeismar-wolfhagen.de
  (Kirchspiel Lippoldsberg = Gewissenruh + Gottstreu + Lippoldsberg + Vernawahlshausen),
  bruecke-online.net.
- **No Klaus Kunze OSB** exists for Gottstreu or Gewissenruh — the HeiKun-Verlag catalogue
  covers Lippoldsberg, Gieselwerder, Vernawahlshausen, Oedelsheim, Heisebeck+Arenborn but
  **not the Waldensian colony villages**. So, unlike the other neighbours, there is **no
  surname-register pre-screen** for the pool; the register had to be read directly.

**Key finding: Gottstreu keeps its OWN church-book series on Archion** (it is *not* folded
into Gewissenruh reg. 219001 or the Lippoldsberg books). Archion → Hessen → Landeskirchl.
Archiv Kurhessen-Waldeck → Kirchenkreis Hofgeismar → **Gottstreu**: KB 1722–1833, **KB
1830–1927**, KB 1928–2010 (+ a 4th). This register had **never been searched** — a genuine
gap in the neighbour ring.

## 3. Verdict

**Gottstreu ELIMINATED.** Two independent tests agree:
1. **Direct 1835 read** — no male born 26 Jan 1835 (only twin Bock girls in January); no Carl.
2. **Alphabetical index** — no pool surname anywhere in the Taufen 1830–~1855, so no
   "Carl/Karl on a pool surname b. 1833–1841" can exist (project elimination criterion met).

This was the **last genuinely un-searched parish in the Lippoldsberg neighbour ring.** The
ring is now complete (Oedelsheim, Gieselwerder, Vernawahlshausen, Gewissenruh, **Gottstreu**,
Bodenfelde, Bursfelde/Glashütte=Fürstenhagen, Wahmbeck) — all negative for a Carl-on-pool
baptism in the window. Wülmersen needs no separate sweep (records are within Deisel/
Trendelburg, already searched). Consistent with Charles having **no recoverable German
baptism** in this Weser corridor (supports the born-/named-after-emigration reading).
