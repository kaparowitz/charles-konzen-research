## Analysis and Implications

### 1. The Konze children's baptisms were recorded elsewhere

Peter Schrader's records document specific birth dates for Maria Sabine (10.1.1834), August (15.10.1834), and other Konze children. These children existed — their confirmation (Maria Carolina, Justus Ernst) and burial records are in the Trendelburg register. Yet their baptisms are absent from the Trendelburg Taufbuch.

**Possible explanations:**

- **Civil birth register vs. church baptism:** Civil registration in Trendelburg did not begin until October 1874, so this is unlikely as a source for Peter's dates.
- **A different church register:** Friedrichsfeld was a Lutheran settler colony. While Trendelburg pastors cared for Friedrichsfeld starting in 1776, it's possible that for a period the Friedrichsfeld baptisms were recorded in a separate sub-register or Nebenbuch not included in register 220108. Archion lists separate "Friedrichsfeld: Church records 1830–2011" and "Friedrichsfeld: Baptisms 1869–2013" — the latter starts in 1869, but the "Church records 1830–2011" could contain earlier baptisms in a mixed register (Kirchenbuch).
- **Peter's data from a different source:** Peter Schrader may have obtained the birth dates from the Hessisches Staatsarchiv Marburg records (e.g., B 33 Geburtenbuch der Stadt Trendelburg, 1849–1857) or from a Familienbuch compiled from multiple sources, not directly from this specific church register.
- **The baptisms are in the earlier register (Kirchenbuch 1819–1831):** Unlikely for children born 1834+, but the transition between registers should be verified.

### 2. Confirmation of the "family departed" timeline

Combined with the Konfirmationen search results (no Konze entries after 1843), the absence of Konze baptisms in the entire 1830–1863 register reinforces that:

- The Konze children's sacramental records at Trendelburg are **incomplete** — confirmations exist (1842, 1843) but baptisms do not.
- The family departed the area between 1843 and 1846.
- Any child born ~1835 (our Charles Gannsen candidate) would have been ~8–11 years old at departure.

### 3. Next steps
