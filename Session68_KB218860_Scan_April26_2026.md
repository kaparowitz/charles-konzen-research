## Four Hildebrand Families in Deisel (1830–1834)

From the systematic KB 218860 scan, four distinct Hildebrand patriarchs are documented as active in Deisel 1830–1834:

| # | Patriarch | Wife | Children documented in KB 218860 |
|---|-----------|------|-----------------------------------|
| 1 | **Christoph Hildebrand** | Barbara Elizabeth geb. Schübl | Daughter Wilhelmine Elisabeth (m. Walter, Entry #23c); Daughter Wilhelmine (m. Töpler, Entry #24); Son Johannes (m. Franke, Entry #27) |
| 2 | **Joh. Heinrich Hildebrand** | Anna Baltz née Olfenberger | Daughter Wilhelmina (m. Brüning, Entry #28) |
| 3 | **Johann Conrad Hildebrand** | Anna Balthaserin née Hofawinch | Son Ludwig (m. Grabe, Entry #41) |
| 4 | **Christian Hildebrand** | Catharina Elisabeth née Schlott/Göbel | Son Ludwig (m. Jordan, earlier entries) |

**Entry 195's Wilhelmine Hildebrand** (mother of Justus Konze, b. January 1835) does NOT appear as a bride among any of these families. She is not one of the documented daughters. This means either:
- She is from a fifth Deisel Hildebrand family not documented in KB 218860 (1830–1834)
- She originates from a neighboring parish (Heisebeck, Schöneberg, or another village) and married into Deisel with Joh. Heinrich Konze
- She married Joh. Heinrich Konze before 1830 (in a register not yet located)

## Key Conclusions

### 1. Joh. Heinrich Konze × Wilhelmine Hildebrand NOT in KB 218860 (entries #1–43)

The marriage did NOT take place in Deisel between July 1830 and August 1834. Since Justus Konze was born January 23–24, 1835, any marriage after April 1834 is biologically too late. Combined with the existing NEGATIVE result for KB 218824 (Deisel 1796–1831), the marriage is NOT in any surviving Deisel register.

**Conclusion: The marriage took place outside of Deisel**, most likely in the parish of origin of Wilhelmine Hildebrand — which is not yet identified. Searches of Heisebeck (KB 219169 + 219190) and Schöneberg (KB 220006 + 220012) in Session 67 were also negative.

### 2. Johann Christoph Konze × Marein Dönner — 1833 date confirmed

Entry #26 gives banns 30 März 1833, copulated 19 Juli 1833. This confirms the 3rd marriage of Joh. Christoph Konze (Bürgermeister) previously listed as "× Maria Drönner." Date now fully documented.

### 3. Andreas Konze — confirmed son of Joh. Christoph × Margaretha Hillebrand

Entry #36 (image 1307) explicitly names Andreas Konze's parents as "Joh. Christoph Konze × Margaretha Hillebrand." This adds Andreas as a documented son of J.C. Konze's 1st marriage (the Hillebrand marriage). He married Marein Elisabeth Grabe in April 1834.

### 4. Grabe family cross-link

Both Andreas Konze (Entry #36) and Ludwig Hildebrand (Entry #41) married women from the Grabe family of Deisel in 1834 — confirming the Grabe family was a significant marriage partner family in Deisel in this period.
