## 9. Deisel Kirchenbuch 1736–1796 (Register 218821) — Burial Section Search

**Register:** 218821, burial section approximately images 1540–1565
**Search method:** Page-by-page examination of images 1555–1561, zoomed in on every entry
**Period covered:** ~1788–1796

### MAJOR DISCOVERY: Andreas Kontze (I) — Burial Found

**Image 1558, right page — Entry 223 (March 1791)**

> **Andreas Kontze, gewesenen Küster** (former sexton)
> † d. 2ten [Martij] dieses Abends 9 Uhr
> Alt 72 Jahr 6 Monat
> Nr. 1

**Translation:** Andreas Kontze, former sexton (church custodian), died March 2, 1791, at 9 PM, aged 72 years 6 months. House Nr. 1 in Deisel.

**Calculated birth:** ~September 1718

**Significance:** This is **the most important single discovery** in the entire research project to date. Andreas Kontze (I) is the patriarch of the Kontze/Konze family in Deisel — father of Johann Friedrich Kontze and grandfather in the line leading to Charles Gannsen/Johnson.

**New information revealed:**
1. **Occupation: Küster (sexton)** — Andreas was the church sexton, a position of some standing in the community requiring literacy. This was not previously known.
2. **Exact death date: March 2, 1791** — Previously only known as "before February 1795"
3. **Exact age at death: 72 years 6 months** — Birth ~September 1718, much earlier than the previously estimated ~1735
4. **House Nr. 1** — The family home in Deisel

**Analytical note on birth year:** The previous estimate of b. ~1735 for Andreas may have actually referred to his son Johann Friedrich's birth. If Andreas was born ~1718 and Konrad Konze (his confirmed son, b. 1780) was born when Andreas was ~62, this is biologically plausible though on the older side. Johann Friedrich Kontze (b. ~1735?) would have been born when Andreas was ~17, which is young but possible — or Johann Friedrich's birth year estimate may need revision.

### SECOND DISCOVERY: Johann Henrich Kontze — Previously Unknown Family Member

> **Johann Henrich Kontze, Ackermann** (farmer)

**Translation:** Johann Henrich Kontze, farmer, buried April 19, 1792; died April 17, 1792, at 6 PM; aged 45 years 25 days.

**Calculated birth:** ~March 23, 1747

**Significance:** Johann Henrich Kontze was born in the same year (~1747) as Johann Christoph Contze (the Grenadier, b. ~1747). He is very likely a **brother** of Johann Christoph — both would be sons of Andreas Kontze (I). His occupation as Ackermann (farmer) contrasts with Johann Christoph's military service as a Grenadier.
