## Session 99 progress (8 June 2026) — txt/HTR workflow established

**Archion txt button works (after Jed topped up 25 Texterkennung credits).** Mechanics learned:
- The "txt" button opens a side panel; on an un-recognized page it shows **"Order text recognition" / "Texterkennung beauftragen"**. Clicking it spends **1 credit** and **queues** the page (asynchronous — result appears in the panel after *several minutes*, longer for ARCHION-reserved images).
- It is NOT instant and NOT free per-view: each page = 1 credit + a processing wait. So txt is for **targeted** reads, not bulk scanning.
- **Stammen has NO usable alphabetical index.** The combined register **220063** title page states "(1775–1831) **ohne Register**"; the burial book **220072** (1830–1936) begins entries immediately after the title with no Namensregister at front or back. Session 97's "K-index" was therefore not a register index — there is no shortcut. Lookups need a **known event-year** (jump chronologically) or a **page anchor**.
- **Visual scanning is free** (screenshots/zoom); only the txt transcription costs a credit. Efficient workflow = navigate + zoom to find the right page for free, THEN spend 1 credit on txt for an accurate reading.
