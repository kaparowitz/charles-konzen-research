## Move 2 — Andreas Kontze (b. 1795) family, eyewitnessed

Two of Andreas's children rendered clearly:

- **Heinrich Wilhelm Kontze** — b. **12 February 1832**; father *Andreas Kontze, Colonist*; mother *Maria Sophia, geborene Hofeditz* (his 2nd wife); godfather *Justus Henckel von Stammen*.
- **August Kontze** — b. **15 October 1834**, baptised **17 October**; same parents; **died** (remarks column); the father *Andreas Kontze himself* presented the child for baptism — the documented "his-own-son's-godfather" pattern.

This independently confirms (from the register, not just the Stammtafel): Andreas Kontze × **Maria Sophia née Hofeditz**, resident as a *Colonist* at Friedrichsfeld, native of Trendelburg. Both children's mothers are **Hofeditz, not Konze** — relevant to the maternal-line ("Konze women") question.

## Move 3 — Tangential find
